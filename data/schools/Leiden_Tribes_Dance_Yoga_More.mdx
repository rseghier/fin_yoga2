# Studio Presentation

This yoga and dance studio offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, inviting individuals from all backgrounds to join in a diverse range of classes. Located in the heart of Leiden, it features a clean and simple environment that enhances the overall experience. Classes are available in English, making it accessible to both locals and internationals. The studio provides a variety of yoga styles, from relaxing yin nidra to challenging flow classes, and also incorporates workshops and dance courses. Despite some negative feedback regarding customer service and workshop notifications, many reviewers highlight the friendly instructors and the community feel, noting that it is a place where people can come as they are and find their vibe.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and cozy atmosphere
- Diverse range of classes including yoga and dance
- Clean and simple studio environment
- Friendly and skilled instructors
- Classes available in English for inclusivity

## Areas for Improvement

- Customer service issues regarding notifications for canceled workshops
- Problems with refunds not being processed
- Some classes perceived as low-intensity
