# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Mumbai offers a mix of unique teachings and personalized attention to its students. Reviews highlight the friendliness of the staff and their dedication to providing exceptional guidance during sessions. Each class begins and ends with prayers and chants, contributing to a spiritual atmosphere. However, there have been complaints regarding the treatment of customers, especially concerning specific health conditions, which some reviewers found insensitive. Despite the issues with staff behavior, many long-time students express satisfaction with their personal growth and learning over the years.

Located conveniently near local landmarks, the studio provides a welcoming space for yogis of all levels.

## Why You'll Love It

- Exceptional teachings and personalized attention from staff
- Friendly and supportive atmosphere
- Spiritual experience with prayers and chants in sessions
- Long-term students report consistent personal growth

## Areas for Improvement

- Rude behavior of staff noted by several reviewers
- Insensitive treatment of customers with health conditions
- Issues with customer service during entry policies
