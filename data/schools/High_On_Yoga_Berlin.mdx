# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming atmosphere ideal for practitioners of all levels, especially beginners. The instructors emphasize improving form and flexibility, providing personalized adjustments during classes. Participants can enjoy a blend of meditation and dynamic movement, particularly through Ashtanga and vinyasa styles. The studio has a vibrant energy, creating an engaging environment for yoga practice. However, some technical issues with online classes have caused inconvenience for some attendees, which has been a downside for their experience.

Overall, it's praised for its affordability and unique classes that can't be found elsewhere in Berlin.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming atmosphere for all experience levels, especially beginners.
- Personalized adjustments to improve practice.
- Unique classes not found anywhere else in Berlin.
- Vibrant energy in the studio.
- Affordable rates for quality yoga instruction.

## Areas for Improvement

- Technical issues with online classes causing frustration.
- Some reports of unprofessionalism from staff.
- Difficulty finding the studio due to unclear signage.
