# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a beautiful and tranquil environment for practitioners, with a focus on various styles including air yoga and vinyasa. Reviews highlight excellent instructors who provide clear guidance and create a welcoming atmosphere. While many enjoy the classes and appreciate the architecture of the building, the studio faces criticism regarding the cleanliness of the provided mats and materials, with several reports of them being dirty and poorly maintained. The reception staff has received mixed feedback, with some patrons describing a lack of attentiveness and friendliness. Despite these concerns, many reviews emphasize the peaceful ambiance and the quality of the classes, making it a recommended spot for both beginners and seasoned yogis.

The studio is located in Cuauhtémoc, Mexico City, offering a unique space for yoga enthusiasts. Its favorable location and architectural charm are also frequently appreciated by visitors.

## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful and tranquil environment
- Excellent instructors with clear guidance
- Variety of classes including air yoga and vinyasa
- Architectural charm of the building
- Highly recommended classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Dirty and poorly maintained yoga mats and props
- Indifferent and unfriendly reception staff
- Lack of cleaning and sanitization after classes
- Space can feel crowded at times
- Mixed experiences regarding customer service
