# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio, located in a serene and somewhat hidden environment, offers a unique blend of traditional yoga practices and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Renowned for its skilled instructors, particularly Nengah, the studio provides personalized attention in small class sizes, ensuring that both beginners and advanced practitioners can thrive. Classes are characterized by detailed teachings and hands-on adjustments, promoting proper technique and a deep understanding of yoga. Students frequently mention how the serene ambiance enhances their experience, contributing to a sense of peace and energy after each session. The absence of air conditioning and the open-air design add to the studio's charm, allowing practitioners to connect with the natural surroundings while enjoying their practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and caring instructors, especially Nengah and Desak.
- Small class sizes allowing for personalized attention.
- Engaging classes suitable for all levels of practitioners.
- Beautiful and tranquil open-air environment.
- Focus on detailed instructions and hands-on adjustments.

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited air conditioning might be uncomfortable for some.
- Location can be tricky to find initially.
- Outdoor setting may not appeal to everyone.
