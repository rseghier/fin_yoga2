# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Berlin is celebrated for its dedicated and knowledgeable instructor, Teju, who has made a lasting impression on her students through her empathetic approach and deep understanding of yoga. Each class is a journey towards renewal and positive energy, with a supportive atmosphere that inspires practitioners of all levels. The diverse range of classes, including Hot Yoga and other styles, allows individuals to experience various techniques and flows. However, while many students find her sessions invigorating and well-rounded, some have expressed concerns about communication issues regarding class availability. The studio fosters a strong sense of community and commitment to its practitioners.

Classes are not only physically challenging but also create a welcoming space for personal growth and reflection, making it a unique destination for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and empathetic instructor
- Classes provide a sense of renewal and positive energy
- Diverse range of yoga styles offered
- Strong community atmosphere
- Well-rounded classes with strategic adjustments

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with class availability and communication
- Cancellation of evening Hot Hatha classes
- Occasional confusion regarding class schedules
