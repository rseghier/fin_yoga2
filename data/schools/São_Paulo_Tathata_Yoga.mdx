# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a rich and immersive experience in Hatha Yoga, with a strong emphasis on traditional practices as taught by Sadhguru. The environment is described as welcoming and conducive to learning, with personal attention given by the instructor, Nikhil, and his wife, Priscila. Students have praised the clarity and objectivity of the classes, making it accessible regardless of their English proficiency. The unique opportunity to learn Angamardana and Surya Kriya in a deeply engaging manner has resulted in transformative experiences for many. The studio combines physical practice with a sense of community and genuine care, facilitating a supportive atmosphere for personal growth.

Many students appreciate the supportive hospitality, including shared meals and accommodations that enhance their learning journey. The instructive approach, combined with enthusiastic guidance, provides an authentic connection to the teachings of Isha, creating a truly enriching yoga experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and attentive instructors who provide personalized attention.
- Immersive and transformative learning experiences in traditional yoga practices.
- Clear and objective instruction that supports various learning levels.
- A warm and pleasant atmosphere that fosters community and personal growth.
- The unique opportunity to learn certified practices directly from a trained instructor.

## Areas for Improvement

- Language barriers for non-English speakers, though support is provided.
- Limited information on the variety of classes offered aside from specific programs.
- Potentially challenging for beginners due to the intensity of some practices.
