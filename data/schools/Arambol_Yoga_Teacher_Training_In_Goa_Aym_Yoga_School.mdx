# Studio Presentation

This yoga school offers a transformative experience for students looking to deepen their practice and knowledge. The instructors are highly experienced and create a supportive environment that encourages personal growth. Courses include a balance of asana, pranayama, philosophy, and anatomy, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of yoga. The food is often praised for its deliciousness, with a variety of vegetarian options available. The school is situated in a serene area, just a short walk from the beach, allowing students to enjoy nature during their downtime. However, accommodations vary, and some rooms may not meet expectations, lacking basic amenities. Overall, the atmosphere is welcoming, and the community fosters learning and connection.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly experienced and supportive instructors
- Delicious and diverse vegetarian food
- Transformative yoga training experience
- Serene location near the beach
- Strong sense of community and support among students

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistent quality of accommodations
- Reported issues with cleanliness in some rooms
- Staff hospitality can be variable
- Internet access issues
- Some courses may not cater well to beginners
