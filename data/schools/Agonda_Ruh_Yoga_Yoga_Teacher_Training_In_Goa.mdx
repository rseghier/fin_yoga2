# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a well-structured 200-hour yoga teacher training program set in a beautiful and peaceful location in Agonda, Goa. The training emphasizes a balanced approach, incorporating theory, practice, and hands-on teaching experience. The educators are knowledgeable, supportive, and create a nurturing environment enriched with laughter and joy. With small class sizes, personalized attention is provided, which fosters deep connections among students and teachers. Accommodations are basic yet adequate for the experience, and meals are provided at a nearby restaurant, known for its tasty dishes. The proximity to the beach and nature enhances the overall experience, making it ideal for self-growth and transformation through yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Well-structured training program with a balance of theory and practice.
- Knowledgeable and supportive teachers who create an inspiring environment.
- Small class sizes allow for personalized attention and connection.
- Beautiful and peaceful location near the beach and nature.
- Focus on holistic yoga education, including philosophy and anatomy.

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited variety in food options over the training period.
- Basic accommodations may not meet luxury expectations.
- Some reviews mention noise issues in certain rooms.
- Physical challenges in the training may not suit all participants.
