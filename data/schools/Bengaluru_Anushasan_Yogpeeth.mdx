# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comprehensive Hatha Yoga Teacher Training Course that emphasizes personalized attention and a holistic approach to yoga. Students appreciate the knowledgeable instructors, particularly Atul, who is known for his patience and supportive guidance. The courses blend theoretical knowledge with practical applications, covering topics such as asana alignment, pranayama techniques, and the philosophical aspects of yoga including the Yoga Sutras and Bhagavad Gita. The atmosphere is described as serene and welcoming, with an airy and light-filled space that enhances the yoga experience. Many reviews highlight the positive impact of individualized instruction, which caters to students of all levels, from beginners to those seeking to deepen their practice. Overall, the studio is recognized for fostering a supportive community and providing effective, structured yoga education.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and approachable instructors, especially Atul.
- Personalized attention given to each student, enhancing learning.
- Comprehensive curriculum that includes theoretical and practical aspects of yoga.
- Warm and welcoming atmosphere, with a focus on individual needs.
- Well-structured classes with flexible scheduling options.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some courses may require prior knowledge of yoga.
- Limited information on post-course support for graduates.
