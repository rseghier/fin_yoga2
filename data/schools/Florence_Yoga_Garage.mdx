# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for practitioners of all levels, whether you are a beginner or an experienced yogi. Classes are taught in both English and Italian, allowing for an inclusive experience. The instructors are described as knowledgeable and attentive, providing personal guidance and hands-on assistance. The studio emphasizes a traditional form of Hatha flow, encouraging a serious approach to practice with minimal chit-chat, creating space for internal work and reflection. While the ambiance is generally praised, it can get crowded, so advanced booking through the MINDBODY app is recommended. Overall, it provides a beautiful setting for a dynamic yet mindful practice amidst the historical charm of Florence.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and attentive instructors
- Classes available in English and Italian
- Inviting and peaceful studio atmosphere
- Challenging classes suitable for all levels
- Beautiful setting with glass ceilings and great views

## Areas for Improvement

- Can be crowded, requiring advance booking
- Some negative experiences reported with customer service
- Teaching may not be suitable for complete beginners
- Inconsistencies in instructions for pose breakdown
- Might feel more fitness-oriented rather than purely yoga-focused
