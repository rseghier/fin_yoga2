# Studio Presentation

This yoga academy offers a transformative experience nestled in the serene nature of Rishikesh, away from the city's hustle and bustle. The intimate class sizes foster personalized attention, allowing each student to receive guidance tailored to their individual needs. The knowledgeable and passionate instructors cover diverse aspects of yoga, from asanas and anatomy to philosophy and meditation, ensuring a well-rounded training experience. Students have highlighted the quality of the food, comfortable lodging, and the picturesque surroundings, which include a nearby waterfall and lush greenery. Many reviewers commend the warm and supportive atmosphere created by the staff, helping everyone feel welcomed and encouraged on their yoga journey. Whether joining for teacher training or retreats, participants leave feeling enriched and uplifted.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and passionate instructors dedicated to student growth
- Small class sizes facilitating personalized guidance
- Beautiful, peaceful location surrounded by nature with a waterfall
- Well-structured courses covering various aspects of yoga
- Delicious and healthy food provided during training

## Areas for Improvement

- Experiences of miscommunication regarding course details
- Some students reported uncomfortable situations in certain classes
- Occasional concerns regarding facilities during adverse weather conditions
- A few negative experiences highlighted regarding refunds and customer service
