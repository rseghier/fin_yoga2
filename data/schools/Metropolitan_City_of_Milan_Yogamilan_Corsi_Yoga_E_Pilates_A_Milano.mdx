# Studio Presentation

Located in Milan, this yoga studio offers a welcoming and friendly environment ideal for practitioners of all levels. The highly qualified instructors deliver unique and enriching classes, ensuring each student feels supported. In addition to yoga, the studio provides Pilates and functional training. The clean and spacious atmosphere fosters maximum relaxation and focus. The studio emphasizes an authentic yoga experience, featuring deep-rooted teachings and transformative retreats, including those in India. Students appreciate the professionalism and kindness of the staff, making it a perfect spot for newcomers and seasoned practitioners alike. Overall, it's a revered center known for its commitment to quality education and community spirit.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and friendly environment for all levels
- Highly qualified and competent instructors
- Clean and spacious facilities
- Offerings of various yoga styles and Pilates
- Strong sense of community and support

## Areas for Improvement

- Some reviews mention unfriendly staff
- Quality of classes perceived as low by some attendees
- Negative experience related to allergic reactions for one visitor
