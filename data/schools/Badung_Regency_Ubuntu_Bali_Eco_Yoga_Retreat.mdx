# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a tranquil retreat from the bustling surroundings of Canggu, Bali, nestled in a lush, green environment that promotes peace and relaxation. The studio emphasizes authenticity, sustainability, and deepening one's yoga practice, led by experienced teachers who create a supportive atmosphere for all levels. With eco-friendly accommodations and attentive staff, visitors can enjoy a variety of yoga classes including Mysore and Ashtanga. The studio also provides holistic wellness options such as coaching and massage therapies. Guests have praised the delightful dining options, emphasizing healthy, organic meals, contributing to a feeling of overall well-being. It's a place for personal growth and connection, making it ideal for both beginners and seasoned practitioners.

## Why You'll Love It

- Peaceful and serene environment surrounded by nature.
- Experienced and knowledgeable instructors offering personalized guidance.
- Supportive and welcoming community feel.
- Holistic approach with additional wellness services such as coaching and massages.
- Delicious and healthy food options available on-site.

## Areas for Improvement

- Unreliable scheduling of yoga classes.
- Inconsistent communication regarding retreat activities.
- Some guests reported issues with noise levels in accommodations.
- Overpriced compared to nearby options.
- Lack of cleaning services during longer stays.
