# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a profound experience in traditional Ashtanga yoga, led by knowledgeable and compassionate instructors who provide personalized attention and adjustments. Students appreciate the intimate and nurturing environment where they can deepen their practice. The studio hosts a range of programs, including intensive courses that blend asana, philosophy, anatomy, and pranayama, urging practitioners to engage both physically and mentally. The beautiful setting enhances the experience, fostering a sense of community among students. However, the studio is noted for its selective entry policy, which may discourage some aspiring practitioners. The dedicated teachers have a remarkable ability to connect with students, making the learning process both challenging and rewarding, ensuring that each individual's journey is valued and supported.

## Why You'll Love It

- Compassionate and knowledgeable instructors who provide personalized guidance.
- High-quality intensive courses that integrate asana, anatomy, and philosophy.
- Beautiful and welcoming environment with great energy.
- Focus on safe alignment and body awareness to avoid injuries.
- Strong sense of community and supportive atmosphere.

## Areas for Improvement

- Selective entry policy that may exclude some practitioners.
- Perceived elitism in class access.
- Some reviews mention a competitive or tense atmosphere.
