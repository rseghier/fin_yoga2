# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of classes, including Barre, Animal Flow, and holistic yoga, catering to various levels and preferences. Reviewers appreciate the knowledgeable and engaging instructors who create an inviting atmosphere. The studio features beautiful spaces that enhance the yoga experience, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and rejuvenation. Additionally, the on-site bistro serves healthy snacks, adding to the appeal for those looking to unwind post-class. However, there have been concerns about communication, particularly regarding class cancellations and the use of Multisport cards for Pilates classes, indicating a need for better organization and clarity in services offered.



## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and engaging instructors
- Beautiful and inviting studio spaces
- On-site bistro offering healthy snacks
- Diverse class offerings including Barre and Animal Flow
- Positive atmosphere for relaxation and rejuvenation

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistent communication regarding class cancellations
- Issues with Multisport card usage for Pilates classes
- Occasional lack of reliability in class scheduling
