# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming environment with a variety of classes tailored to different skill levels. Instructors are highly praised for their expertise, humor, and ability to make classes both challenging and enjoyable. The studio features clean facilities and convenient amenities like lockers and a changing room, making it easy for students to transition to post-class activities. The atmosphere is described as warm and friendly, with a strong sense of community. Many reviewers noted the studio's excellent location near public transit, contributing to its reputation as a neighborhood favorite. Overall, this studio is committed to providing a supportive and enriching yoga experience.



## Why You'll Love It

- Highly skilled and personable instructors
- Welcoming and friendly atmosphere
- Clean facilities and convenient amenities
- Great location near TTC Lawrence Station
- Unique class offerings with varied challenges

## Areas for Improvement

- Strict on punctuality; late entries may not be allowed
- Some religious iconography may be off-putting for certain attendees
- Instances of cultural appropriation concerns noted by some
