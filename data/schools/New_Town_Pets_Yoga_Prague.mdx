**Address:** Senovážné nám. 978, 110 00 Nové Město, Czechia

**Category:** Yoga studio

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a unique experience that combines yoga with the joy of interacting with adorable puppies. Many reviews highlight the friendly instructors and a welcoming atmosphere, making it suitable for beginners and dog lovers alike. While participants appreciate the chance to practice yoga, some express concerns about overcrowding during classes, which can limit their ability to engage with the puppies. The environment is generally described as clean and well-organized, with attentive staff who provide guidance and information about the puppies. However, the yoga sessions sometimes prioritize relaxation and puppy interaction over intense stretching, leading to mixed feelings about the overall value. Despite these drawbacks, many attendees leave with positive memories and the desire to return for more classes.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and welcoming atmosphere
- Great for beginners and dog lovers
- Well-organized classes with attentive staff
- Clean and comfortable environment
- Unique experience combining yoga and puppy interaction

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes can be overcrowded, limiting puppy interaction
- Some yoga sessions may not be suitable for pregnant participants
- Higher price point compared to traditional yoga classes
- Yoga focus may be light, with an emphasis on relaxation
- Confusion regarding the booking process with gift vouchers
