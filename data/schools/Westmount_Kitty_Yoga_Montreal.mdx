Kitty Yoga, located in Montreal, combines gentle yoga with kitten interaction for a unique experience.

**Address:** 4823 Sherbrooke St W suite 100, Westmount, Quebec H3Z 1G7, Canada

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 3.4 based on 16 reviews

**Website:** https://kittyyogamtl.com/

**Phone:** +1 514-427-1915

# Studio Presentation

The studio offers a unique blend of yoga and kitten interaction, allowing participants to enjoy gentle yoga while surrounded by adorable cats. Reviews indicate that the atmosphere is warm and welcoming, with instructors who are friendly and engaging. However, the space is limited, leading to crowding and difficulty in movement during sessions, which adversely affects the relaxation aspect. Many feel that while the concept is charming, the execution falls short, with too many participants for the space available and a lack of substantial yoga practice in the sessions, often more meditative than physically engaging.
The experience aims to support cat rescue organizations, which is appreciated by some reviewers, but concerns about overcrowding and the quality of sessions are prevalent. Improvements in organization and communication around session dynamics are suggested for a more fulfilling experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and engaging instructors
- Cozy and warm atmosphere
- Unique concept combining yoga with kitten interaction
- Therapeutic experience appreciated by some
- Support for cat rescue organizations

## Areas for Improvement

- Crowded sessions making relaxation difficult
- Limited space with too many participants
- Sessions often more focused on meditation than on traditional yoga
- Poor quality mats and equipment
- Inadequate communication about the session structure
