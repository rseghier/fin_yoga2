# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and welcoming environment for practitioners of Ashtanga Yoga. It is appreciated for its beautiful shala and for the supportive community cultivated by the teachers. Classes, such as 'Ashtanga Prep,' are known for their good instruction, detailed attention to breath and anatomy, and accommodating approaches for varying skill levels through the use of modifications. The teaching style combines traditional Ashtanga methods with a family-like atmosphere, making it accessible to both Spanish and English speakers. Overall, the studio promotes a sense of peace and authenticity, making it an ideal place for both learning and practicing yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and welcoming atmosphere
- High-quality instruction with a focus on breath and anatomy
- Supportive and family-like community
- Accommodating teaching style with modifications for varying skill levels
