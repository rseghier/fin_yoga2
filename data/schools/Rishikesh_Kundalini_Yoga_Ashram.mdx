# Studio Presentation

Located in the heart of Rishikesh, this yoga studio offers a transformative experience focused on Kundalini Yoga. The environment is enriched by the stunning views of the Ganga River and the majestic Himalayas, providing a serene backdrop for spiritual journeys and intensive training. The staff and teachers are described as knowledgeable, kind, and supportive, fostering a sense of community among students. Students often highlight the depth of content available in the curriculum, along with a nurturing atmosphere. While the meals are praised for their quality, and the accommodation is generally acceptable, there are observations regarding discipline and cleanliness that suggest areas for improvement. Overall, the studio is dedicated to personal growth and the genuine practice of yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and passionate teachers
- Beautiful location with views of the Ganga and Himalayas
- Strong sense of community among students
- High-quality meals prepared by the staff
- Deep, transformative curriculum

## Areas for Improvement

- Discipline issues during classes
- Concerns about cleanliness of facilities
- Limited diversity in yoga styles offered
- Inconsistent support for student needs
- Some negative experiences with certain staff members
