Yoga Light is a cozy yoga studio located in Mong Kok.

**Address:** Unit 11, 10/F, Pakpolee Commercial Centre, 1A Sai Yeung Choi St S, Mong Kok, Hong Kong

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.2 based on 41 reviews

**Website:** http://www.yogalight.co/

**Phone:** +852 9511 0111

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a range of classes led by skilled instructors, particularly noting Master Kunal for his clear guidance and ability to cater to different skill levels. The environment is generally described as cozy and accommodating, with good accessibility and facilities such as showers and changing areas. Some students report challenges due to the perceived strictness and occasional meanness from instructors, which can create discomfort for newcomers. Positive reviews highlight a supportive atmosphere in advanced classes, where teachers push students to improve while maintaining a fun and energetic vibe. However, there are concerns regarding late class cancellations that disrupt students' schedules.

## Why You'll Love It

- Skilled and experienced instructors, especially Master Kunal.
- Cozy and welcoming environment with good facilities.
- Classes cater to various skill levels and provide clear instructions.
- Positive energy in classes, making yoga enjoyable.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some instructors display a mean attitude towards struggling students.
- Late cancellation of classes can disrupt student schedules.
- Occasional feelings of unwelcomeness for newcomers.
