# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comfortable and clean environment, making it an inviting place for practitioners. The instructor is noted for being patient and thorough, taking time to demonstrate poses and correct students' mistakes, which enhances the learning experience. Classes sometimes extend beyond the scheduled time, reflecting the instructor's commitment to ensuring students understand the material. Located on the second floor, accessing the studio involves a small climb, which some find serves as a warm-up before class. Parking is available on the street, although it may be limited during certain periods, such as Ramadan.

## Why You'll Love It

- Clean and comfortable studio environment
- Patient and thorough instructor
- Classes occasionally extend for better learning
- Convenient street parking available

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited parking during Ramadan
