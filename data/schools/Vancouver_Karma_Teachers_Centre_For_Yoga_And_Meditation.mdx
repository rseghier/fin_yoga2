# Studio Presentation

Located in Vancouver, this yoga studio offers an intimate setting for its 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training (YTT) program, limited to just 8 students for personalized attention. The instructors are knowledgeable and genuinely care about student growth, fostering a supportive community. Reviews highlight the engaging content that delves into yoga philosophy, anatomy, and teaching methods. Students appreciate the focus on individual progress, with particular mention of the instructors' positive and energetic approach. Many express deep gratitude for the healing experiences and connections made during their training.

Overall, the studio provides quality education with ample opportunities for self-reflection and personal growth, making it a highly recommended choice for both aspiring teachers and those looking to deepen their practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Personalized attention due to small class sizes (limited to 8 students)
- Knowledgeable and caring instructors
- Engaging content covering yoga philosophy and anatomy
- Positive and energetic atmosphere
- Strong community connections among students
