# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comprehensive Teacher Training Course that emphasizes not only physical practice but also the philosophy and lifestyle of yoga. Many reviews highlight the knowledgeable instructors and the supportive community, creating an enriching atmosphere for students. While the courses are well-structured and engaging, there are concerns about the communication prior to arrival and discrepancies between the advertised and actual facilities. The surroundings can be noisy and congested, which detracts from the serene experience one might expect in a spiritual setting. Though there are challenges with the location and accommodations, the quality of instruction and the delicious, healthy meals received high praise from participants. Overall, the studio is recognized for its genuine community vibe and transformative teaching.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and compassionate instructors.
- Well-organized and comprehensive course structure.
- Delicious, healthy meals provided.
- Supportive community atmosphere.
- Small class sizes allowing for personalized attention.

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistent communication prior to arrival.
- Discrepancy between advertised location and actual facilities.
- No heating and uncomfortable accommodations during winter.
- Surroundings can be noisy and congested.
- Lack of trust due to unexpected changes in location.
