# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming environment, particularly suitable for individuals over 60. It features an excellent instructor who enhances the practice with love and dedication. The studio is praised for its highly qualified professionals teaching a variety of yoga disciplines. However, some reviews express dissatisfaction with the administrative decisions, particularly regarding price increases that do not take the financial situation of retirees into account. Overall, it is highly recommended for its quality of instruction and supportive atmosphere.



## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming environment for seniors
- Highly qualified and loving instructor
- Offers a variety of yoga disciplines
- Recommended for high-quality instruction

## Areas for Improvement

- Recent price increases perceived as unreasonable for retirees
- Administrative decisions not considerate of specific clientele
