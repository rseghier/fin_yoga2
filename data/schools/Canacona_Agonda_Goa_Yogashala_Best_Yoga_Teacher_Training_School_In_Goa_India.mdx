# Studio Presentation

Nestled in the tranquil surroundings of South Goa, this yoga school offers a serene and immersive experience for yoga teacher training. With a focus on multi-style yoga, the program includes Hatha, Ashtanga, Vinyasa, Yin, philosophy, and Ayurveda, allowing for a comprehensive education in yoga practices. The warm atmosphere, facilitated by experienced and dedicated instructors, fosters a sense of community that feels like family. The stunning location, just moments from the beach, enhances the experience with beautiful jungle views and opportunities for outdoor activities and excursions. Accommodation is comfortable, with many options featuring air conditioning, and the food is consistently praised for its delicious, varied offerings. This school not only serves as a place of learning but also creates a space for personal growth and lifelong friendships.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and welcoming atmosphere with a strong sense of community.
- Experienced and dedicated teachers, particularly Praveen.
- Diverse curriculum covering multiple yoga styles and wellness practices.
- Delicious and varied vegetarian meals prepared with care.
- Beautiful location near Agonda Beach, enhancing relaxation and experience.

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited variety in class types possibly disappointing some students.
- Basic accommodations for some preferences potentially not meeting all guest expectations.
- Intense schedule may be challenging for some trainees.
