# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a variety of classes and a peaceful atmosphere, making it a desired location for many. It features stunning views from the 20th floor, a meditation room, changing facilities, and an overall inviting vibe. However, frustrations arise from inflexible management and customer service. Customers have reported poor communication regarding class schedules and an unwelcoming attitude from certain instructors, undermining the relaxing environment one would expect from a yoga practice. While some attendees appreciate the space and consider it a healing destination, others have expressed disappointment with management's handling of issues, suggesting there is work needed to improve customer satisfaction.


## Why You'll Love It

- Stunning views from the 20th floor
- Wide variety of classes available
- Inviting and healing atmosphere
- Well-equipped with yoga props and facilities
- Positive experiences noted by long-time attendees

## Areas for Improvement

- Inflexible management regarding class packages
- Poor customer service and communication
- Negative experiences reported with certain instructors
- Lack of responsiveness in handling complaints
