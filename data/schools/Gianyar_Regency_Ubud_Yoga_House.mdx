# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and inviting atmosphere nestled among picturesque rice fields. It is renowned for its experienced instructors, particularly Nicole and Ketut, who create dynamic and engaging classes tailored to all levels. The focus on intimate class sizes ensures personalized attention, allowing students to feel supported in their practice. Many reviews highlight the warmth and friendliness of the staff, contributing to a welcoming vibe that makes everyone feel at home. The scenic surroundings, coupled with classes covering various styles such as Vinyasa, Iyengar, and Yin, provide a holistic approach to yoga. The studio also offers a unique yoga teacher training program, emphasizing individual growth and community support.

Classes range from invigorating sunrise sessions to relaxing restorative ones, catering to diverse needs and preferences. Frequent participants appreciate the peaceful setting and the sense of authenticity that distinguishes this studio from larger, commercial venues. Overall, it promises a nurturing environment for both newcomers and seasoned practitioners alike.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and friendly instructors, particularly Nicole and Ketut.
- Intimate class sizes allowing for personalized attention.
- Beautiful and serene location amidst picturesque rice fields.
- Diverse range of yoga styles taught, including Vinyasa, Iyengar, and Yin.
- Warm and welcoming atmosphere with supportive staff.

## Areas for Improvement

- Occasional overcrowding in classes.
- Limited inclusion of meals during teacher training.
- Lack of ventilation in the studio making it hot at times.
- Transport may be inconvenient for those not staying nearby.
- Some reviewers noted that certain instructors spoke too much during sessions.
