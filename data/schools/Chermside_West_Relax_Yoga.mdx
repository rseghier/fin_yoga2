# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and welcoming environment, making it a sanctuary for practitioners. The knowledgeable instructor, Tes, integrates breathwork with asana practice, fostering a deeper connection between mind, body, and breath. With a variety of classes suitable for all levels, the studio caters to different needs, including live online sessions. Many reviewers appreciate the restorative aspect of the sessions, highlighting the excellent monthly restorative yoga class. The studio is praised for its friendly and inclusive atmosphere, where all ages and abilities feel welcome. Community and nourishment are central themes, with workshops that extend the learning beyond physical yoga practice. Students express a strong attachment to the studio, often feeling a sense of loss when they have to relocate.
