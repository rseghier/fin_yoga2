# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of classes, making it a great alternative to traditional gym workouts. With a clean and spacious environment, it provides easy booking and plenty of space for practitioners. The instructors are praised for their expertise, guiding classes that cater to various experience levels. The warm and welcoming atmosphere of the studio enhances the overall yoga experience, allowing students to thrive in both challenging flow classes and therapeutic yin sessions. Although some have noted a decrease in heat levels in classes post-COVID, the studio still maintains a cozy vibe and well-structured classes that are suitable for all. Its convenient location in the Jordaan neighborhood makes it a popular choice for both locals and visitors.

## Why You'll Love It

- Diverse range of classes available.
- Expert and approachable instructors.
- Welcoming and warm atmosphere.
- Clean and spacious studio with showers.
- Convenient location in the heart of Jordaan.

## Areas for Improvement

- Recent decrease in heat levels in classes.
- Lack of lockers for securing valuables.
- Some first-time visitors feel confused about the setup.
- Limited personal interaction for beginners regarding injuries.
