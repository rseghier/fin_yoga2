# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a holistic retreat experience in a serene environment, facing the Ganga River. It features experienced teachers who guide students through various styles, including Hatha, Ashtanga, and Kundalini yoga. The instruction is personalized, catering to all skill levels from beginners to advanced practitioners. Courses are enriched with Ayurveda treatments and balanced meals. Reviewers highlight the intimate atmosphere created by the family-run business, enhancing the experience of comfort and support. Transformational retreats integrate yoga, meditation, and healing practices. Many visitors express gratitude for the unique blend of challenging yet gentle instruction, thoughtful accommodations, and the welcoming nature of the staff, making it feel like home. The overall experience encourages personal growth and rejuvenation, with many students returning again and again.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and knowledgeable teachers who provide personalized attention
- Holistic approach combining yoga, meditation, and Ayurveda treatments
- Welcoming and comfortable family-run atmosphere
- Delicious, home-cooked Ayurvedic meals
- Variety of yoga styles available for all skill levels

## Areas for Improvement

- Some reported dissatisfaction with individual massage experiences
- A few classes may feel shorter than expected for the price
- Limited information available on specific class schedules or pricing
- Potential challenges in accessing the studio if not staying nearby
