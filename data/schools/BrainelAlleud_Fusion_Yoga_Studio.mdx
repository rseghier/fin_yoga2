# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio presents a mix of experiences from its clients. There are mentions of a strong professional coaching approach with an instructor who is dedicated, knowledgeable, and attentive to students' needs. Many have noted improvements in their flexibility and overall wellbeing, attributing their progress to the personalized guidance provided during classes. The atmosphere is described as clean and pleasant, contributing to a positive experience for many participants. However, some clients have expressed concerns regarding issues like unpleasant odors in the studio and unclear session policies, which have led to frustrations for those who lost session credits due to time constraints. Overall, while there are significant strengths in the coaching quality, there is room for improvement in studio environment and client communication.

## Why You'll Love It

- Professional and knowledgeable instructor
- Notable improvements in students' flexibility
- Clean and pleasant environment
- Classes start on time
- Personalized assistance and corrections during poses

## Areas for Improvement

- Unpleasant odors in the studio
- Lack of clear information regarding session expiration
- Frustrations related to lost session credits
- Difficult positions leading to physical strain
- Some previous negative reviews have been erased
