# Studio Presentation

This studio offers an extensive range of Pilates training, including Reformer and Mat classes. The environment encourages a supportive and collective learning experience with highly competent instructors. Many reviews highlight the engaging and practical nature of the instructor-led courses, making them popular among both aspiring and experienced practitioners. However, the atmosphere has been criticized for being unpleasant due to odor issues. The management quality has also received negative feedback, specifically concerning customer service and scheduling flexibility, which detracted from the overall experience for some attendees. Despite these criticisms, the studio is noted for its modern facilities and the dedication of its teaching staff, which can enhance both learning and practice.

Many reviewers appreciate the organized nature of classes, adhere to a structured training format, and express satisfaction with the knowledge gained during their training.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly competent and dedicated instructors, especially noted is Carina for her engaging teaching style.
- A supportive learning environment with practical training and small group sizes.
- Modern and clean facilities that foster a positive training experience.

## Areas for Improvement

- Staff and management have been criticized for rudeness and poor customer service.
- Physical ambiance issues include unpleasant smells that affect the training environment.
- Scheduling and flexibility issues with booking and class participation.
