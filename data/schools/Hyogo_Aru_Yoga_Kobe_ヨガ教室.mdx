# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming environment with a small class size, allowing for personalized instruction. 
Students of all levels, from beginners to experienced practitioners, receive attentive guidance in a safe and supportive atmosphere. 
Classes are scheduled weekly, making it easier for students to integrate them into their routines. 
The instructor is noted for their bright and gentle teaching style, ensuring that safety and correct poses are emphasized throughout the lessons. 
Participants enjoy the engaging experience, feeling encouraged and supported regardless of their flexibility or skill level.

## Why You'll Love It

- Small class sizes allow for personalized attention.
- Instructor's bright and gentle teaching style.
- Classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced practitioners.
- Focus on safety and injury prevention.
- Flexible scheduling makes it easier to attend.

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited class times, currently only on Monday mornings.
- Some students wish for more class options on weekends.
- Participants who can only attend sporadically may find attendance challenging.
