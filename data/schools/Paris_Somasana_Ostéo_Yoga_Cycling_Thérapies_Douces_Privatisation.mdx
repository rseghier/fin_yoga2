A welcoming yoga studio in Paris with diverse classes but marred by service issues.

**Address:** 17 Rue d'Aumale, 75009 Paris, France

**Category:** Yoga retreat center

**Score:** 4.2 based on 25 reviews

**Website:** https://www.somasana.fr/

**Phone:** +33 1 86 95 09 61

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of yoga classes and therapeutic treatments in a welcoming atmosphere. Many reviews highlight the quality of instruction and the friendly demeanor of the teaching staff. However, some patrons have pointed out significant issues with the front desk staff, particularly regarding their unprofessional and unpleasant attitude, which has detracted from the overall experience. Some clients expressed disappointment with miscommunication about available classes, particularly the discontinuation of certain offerings. While the yoga classes, such as aerial yoga, received positive comments, the negative experiences related to reception overshadowed these positives for several visitors. Overall, it is a place with potential but hampered by poor service at the entrance.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and professional instructors
- Warm and friendly atmosphere in classes
- Diverse range of yoga options offered
- Complimentary tea provided after classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Unpleasant behavior from front desk staff
- Miscommunication about class availability
- Negative experiences reported regarding reception
- Atmosphere can feel uncomfortable due to service issues
