# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, emphasizing a strong sense of community among teachers and students. Located just two blocks from Dundarave Beach in West Vancouver, it provides easy access and ample free parking. The studio is boutique and privately owned, allowing for smaller class sizes where personal attention is prioritized. Students appreciate the well-equipped space, as all necessary props are provided at no charge. The experienced instructors, trained in the Iyengar style, are committed to offering tailored modifications suitable for varying skill levels, fostering a supportive environment for everyone. The surrounding area features fabulous coffee shops and restaurants, enhancing the overall experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Strong community feel among teachers and students
- Small class sizes for personalized attention
- Well-equipped studio with free props available
- Experienced instructors providing tailored modifications
- Convenient location with ample free parking
