# Studio Presentation

Located in Vikhroli, this yoga studio is noted for its knowledgeable and dedicated instructors, particularly Rohit and Rahul, who engage with students on a personal level, fostering a positive and enriching learning environment. Students appreciate the detailed guidance and personal attention given amidst a vibrant atmosphere, making yoga not just an exercise but a pathway to self-discovery and well-being. ## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and dedicated instructors like Rahul and Rohit.
- Personal attention and individual guidance for students.
- Positive and vibrant learning atmosphere.
- Commitment to traditional yoga practices enriched with modern understanding.
- Long-standing reputation as one of the oldest yoga centers in Mumbai.
