# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a transformative teacher training experience set in a stunning natural environment. Nestled in the rice fields near Ubud, it features exceptional instructors who are knowledgeable, supportive, and passionate about sharing their expertise in a variety of yoga styles including Ashtanga and Vinyasa. The curriculum is well-structured and holistic, covering important topics like philosophy, anatomy, pranayama, and meditation. Students appreciate the welcoming atmosphere, which fosters deep connections and a sense of community among participants. The venue includes comfortable accommodation, delicious vegetarian food, and group excursions to local cultural sites. Despite some maintenance needs, the enchanting surroundings and dedicated staff create an unforgettable experience. Whether for beginners or experienced yogis, this studio is highly recommended for anyone seeking an enriching yoga journey.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and supportive instructors.
- Beautiful, serene location amidst nature.
- Well-structured and comprehensive curriculum.
- Delicious vegetarian food and great hospitality.
- Strong sense of community and connection among students.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some maintenance needed at the resort.
- Accommodation may not meet everyone's expectations.
