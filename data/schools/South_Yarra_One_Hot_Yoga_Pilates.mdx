# Studio Presentation

Located in South Yarra, this yoga and Pilates studio is recognized for its welcoming environment and diverse class offerings, catering to all skill levels. With expert instructors who prioritize personalized attention and injury considerations, it provides a holistic approach to wellness. Facilities include high-quality mats, props, and clean amenities. However, some reviews highlight issues with accessibility and customer service, particularly for those with hearing impairments. While the studio boasts an energizing atmosphere and a strong community feel, operational challenges, such as locked doors and lack of staff presence at key times, can detract from the overall experience. Despite these inconveniences, many patrons express love for the classes and instructors, particularly in Pilates reformer training.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and energizing atmosphere
- Expert instructors who provide personalized attention
- High-quality facilities and equipment
- Wide variety of classes offered
- Strong community feel among members

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with accessibility and support for those with hearing impairments
- Frequent instances of locked doors and lack of staff presence
- Difficulties with communication for those arriving just on time
- High pricing compared to nearby studios
- Poor customer service and responsiveness
