# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Berlin is renowned for its supportive environment, with instructors like Claudine and Sita creating a nurturing space for both emotional and physical growth. Students appreciate the caring and talented teachers who provide good instruction in English, emphasizing a considerate approach to yoga practice. However, experiences vary, as there have been instances of miscommunication regarding personal boundaries, particularly concerning touch during classes. The studio’s unique system with 'YES' and 'NO' cards for consent indicates an awareness of the importance of personal comfort, though adherence to this system has been questioned. Overall, it is a place where students can explore yoga in a mindful community setting.


## Why You'll Love It

- Supportive environment fostered by caring and talented teachers.
- Good yoga instruction available in English.
- Unique consent system with 'YES' and 'NO' cards.

## Areas for Improvement

- Instances of disregard for students' consent regarding physical touch.
- Miscommunication regarding class teacher assignments.
- Some negative experiences related to inappropriate actions by instructors.
