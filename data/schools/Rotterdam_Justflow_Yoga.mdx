# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a balanced approach to yoga classes, blending challenge with relaxation. The instructor has a strong background in sports and anatomy, which enhances the quality of the instruction, making it suitable for various skill levels. The studio is praised for its inclusive atmosphere, allowing diverse groups to participate. Additionally, the yin yoga classes provided by other teachers are highly recommended by attendees. However, the studio has faced criticism for its management, particularly regarding customer service experiences that some find unwelcoming. Issues with class sizes have also been mentioned, as too many participants are allowed in a small space, impacting the overall practice experience.

Overall, the studio possesses a strong instructional foundation and a friendly vibe, though improvements in customer service and class capacity management could enhance the experience further.

## Why You'll Love It

- Qualified instructor with a background in sports and anatomy.
- Classes are suitable for various skill levels.
- Inclusive atmosphere attracting a diverse group of participants.
- Highly recommended yin yoga classes.
- Balanced approach between challenge and relaxation.

## Areas for Improvement

- Perceived unwelcoming behavior from the owner.
- Issues with customer service during class sign-up.
- Overcrowded classes in a small space.
- Miscommunication regarding class availability.
- Lack of apologies for misunderstandings.
