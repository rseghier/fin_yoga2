# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and welcoming environment, harmoniously located next to lush rice fields and a tranquil river. Students appreciate the attentive instructors who provide personalized guidance and create a peaceful atmosphere conducive to relaxation and meditation. Classes are varied and suitable for all levels, with particular mention of engaging sessions like Chakra Flow and Vinyasa Flow, highlighted by calming natural sounds that enhance the experience. Many visitors note the intimate class sizes, allowing for individual adjustments and a sense of community. Amenities like refreshing towels and herbal tea after each session further add to the overall appeal, making it a cherished destination for both newcomers and seasoned yogis.

Despite its wonderful ambiance, some reviews suggest improvements could be made regarding the class registration setup and a better policy on filming during sessions. Overall, it stands out as a hidden gem in Canggu, attracting those looking for authenticity in their yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful location next to rice fields and a river
- Attentive and skilled instructors providing personalized guidance
- Small class sizes fostering a community atmosphere
- Variety of classes suitable for different skill levels
- Welcoming and peaceful environment

## Areas for Improvement

- Distractions from late arrivals and registration setup inside the practice area
- Filming during classes can disrupt the experience for some
- Limited class offerings could be expanded
- Some yoga mats are reported to be worn
- Customer service regarding package refunds could be improved
