# Studio Presentation

This yoga and pilates studio offers a serene and aesthetically pleasing environment, ideal for relaxation away from the bustling city. 
It is conveniently located in South Jakarta, near key business areas and public transportation, making it easily accessible. 
The studio is known for its cleanliness, cozy atmosphere, and accommodating staff, contributing to a welcoming experience for all practitioners. 
While the classes are highly praised, especially for group pilates reformer sessions and private wellness sessions, there are complaints regarding the customer service and booking process, revealing some inconsistencies. 
The ambiance and design have been described as beautiful and calming, making it a favorite for many students.

## Why You'll Love It

- Serene and calming atmosphere
- Convenient location with easy accessibility
- Clean and aesthetically pleasing environment
- Accommodating and friendly staff
- Highly recommended for pilates reformer classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with customer service attitude and administration process
- Confusion regarding booking slots leading to double bookings
- Lack of apologies or acknowledgment of mistakes
