# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming environment for practitioners of all levels, from beginners to advanced yogis, with classes available both indoors and outdoors that embody a serene, Balinese atmosphere. The instructors are highly regarded for their friendliness and attentiveness, making it an ideal space for anyone looking to learn or deepen their practice. The studio provides various class schedules throughout the day, including early morning sessions. Although praised for its cozy ambiance and professional teachers, the facility faces challenges such as overcrowding during peak hours, cleanliness issues in the bathrooms, and a small parking area that can create logistical concerns for visitors. Despite these drawbacks, the studio remains a beloved spot for yoga enthusiasts seeking both community and tranquility amidst the busy backdrop of Jakarta.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and friendly teachers and staff.
- Diverse class offerings for all skill levels.
- Serene, Balinese-inspired ambiance.
- Affordable pricing for yoga sessions.
- Multiple indoor and outdoor class options.

## Areas for Improvement

- Overcrowding during peak class times.
- Cleanliness issues, particularly in the bathrooms.
- Limited parking space.
- Some complaints about students reserving spots for late arrivals.
- Need for better facility maintenance.
