# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers an exceptional experience in a stunning location. Nestled in a garden shala with some ocean view, participants enjoy the calming sound of waves during their practice. The instructors are knowledgeable, professional, and create thoughtful sequences, making each session memorable. Reviews highlight the studio's peaceful atmosphere and attentive service, with yoga mats and other necessary tools provided for convenience. The ability for walk-in sessions and the availability of facilities such as a luxury shower adds to the overall experience. However, it appears the studio caters primarily to tourists and drop-ins, which may affect the communal atmosphere.

## Why You'll Love It

- Stunning location with ocean views and the sound of waves during classes.
- Knowledgeable and professional instructors delivering thoughtful sequences.
- Convenient daily morning classes with walk-in availability.
- Calming atmosphere surrounded by nature and a garden shala.
- Provision of yoga gear like mats and towels for participants.

## Areas for Improvement

- The class atmosphere felt a bit impersonal and less communal.
- Limited information about class types or teachers before arrival.
- Some guests experienced issues with mosquitoes during classes.
- Lack of incentive for outside guests to revisit.
- Payment preferences lean towards cash, as card transactions may be more expensive.
