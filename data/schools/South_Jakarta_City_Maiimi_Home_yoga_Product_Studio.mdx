# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in South Jakarta offers a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere, making it particularly suitable for beginners. With a clean space and comprehensive amenities, it provides everything one needs for a pleasant yoga experience. The instructors are noted for their patience and attention to detail, ensuring a supportive learning environment. Although there were some concerns about the responsiveness of the booking admin, the overall community vibe and the storytelling aspect during classes add to the studio's charm. With classes that cater to various levels, this studio is gaining popularity, especially due to its convenient location near public transport.

The space also features a product studio upstairs, enhancing the overall experience for visitors looking to relax or shop after their yoga session.

## Why You'll Love It

- Comfortable and welcoming atmosphere
- Patient and detail-oriented instructors
- Beginner-friendly classes
- Good amenities and cleanliness
- Convenient location close to public transport

## Areas for Improvement

- Booking administration is slow to respond
- Instances of schedule changes without notice
- High demand for beginner classes leading to quick slot availability
