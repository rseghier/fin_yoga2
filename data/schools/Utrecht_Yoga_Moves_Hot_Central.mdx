# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a pleasant atmosphere and a supportive community focused on the well-being of its members. With a strong emphasis on professional and experienced instructors, students can enjoy a variety of classes, particularly the unique hot 90 sessions that cater to various needs, including those with long-term injuries. However, the studio's new management has implemented a cancellation policy for under-attended classes, which has caused concern among students, particularly those who rely on consistent scheduling. Many have expressed the need for more inclusive practices, such as proper ventilation and a sensory-friendly environment, to accommodate individuals with specific health needs. Overall, the studio maintains a strong reputation for high-quality instruction and an inviting environment, making it a favored choice for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and professional teachers
- Pleasant and inviting atmosphere
- Variety of classes available, particularly hot yoga
- Convenient location

## Areas for Improvement

- Cancellation policy for under-attended classes causing scheduling anxiety
- Lack of inclusivity measures for members with specific health needs
- Request for quieter environments and proper ventilation not fully addressed
