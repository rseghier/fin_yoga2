# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene atmosphere with excellent customer service. Located in a hidden space in Palermo, it features large windows and a surprising amount of room for activities. The studio provides a broad variety of classes suitable for different preferences, ensuring everyone can find a style that resonates with them. The instructors are praised for their expertise, particularly in 'Integral Yoga' classes. Travelers appreciate the free use of mats and props, and the friendly staff contribute to a welcoming environment. While the classes can be hot during summer, the overall experience remains positive, with small class sizes fostering a sense of community.



## Why You'll Love It

- Excellent customer service and friendly staff.
- A wide variety of classes available.
- Spacious studio with large windows.
- Small class sizes promoting a sense of community.
- Free use of mats and props.

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes can get hot in the summer.
- Some class durations exceeded what was advertised.
- Classes only offered in Spanish, with limited English support.
