# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a transformative 200-hour teacher training program that emphasizes a holistic understanding of yoga. 
The experienced instructors, Hardik and Prutha, provide a blend of practical and theoretical knowledge, focusing on yoga anatomy and philosophy rather than merely perfecting asanas. 
They foster an inclusive and supportive atmosphere, making yoga accessible to everyone, regardless of their level of experience. 
Students appreciate their gentle teaching style and the sense of community they cultivate, often forming lasting connections with the instructors and fellow trainees. 
The program is well-structured, offering a comprehensive curriculum that encourages personal growth and a deeper appreciation for yoga's complexities.
