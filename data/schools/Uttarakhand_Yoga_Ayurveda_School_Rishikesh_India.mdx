# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of classes focused on both yoga and Ayurveda, emphasizing health and well-being. The instructor is noted for being highly skilled, dedicated, and humble, providing personalized attention and support to students. The atmosphere is described as clean, spacious, and supportive, perfect for retreats and mantra learning. Many reviews highlight the variety and challenge of the classes, which incorporate breathing exercises and alternative postures, catering to practitioners of all levels. The studio has become a favored destination for both local and international yoga enthusiasts seeking a transformative experience in a serene environment.

Overall, the studio is lauded for its retreats, yoga alignment classes, and the quality of Ayurveda treatments, making it a premier choice in Rishikesh for those looking to deepen their practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly skilled and humble instructor with personalized attention
- Clean and spacious environment
- Variety of interesting and challenging classes
- Focus on health and well-being through yoga and Ayurveda
- Popular for retreats and mantra learning

## Areas for Improvement

- Some reviews mention it can get hot inside
- Difficulty in finding the studio for first-time visitors
