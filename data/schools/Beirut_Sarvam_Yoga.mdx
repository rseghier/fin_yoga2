# Studio Presentation

Located in the vibrant city of Beirut, this yoga studio is known for its supportive and knowledgeable instructors who create a welcoming atmosphere for all practitioners, from beginners to advanced yogis. The studio not only offers a diverse range of classes but also regularly hosts transformative retreats and workshops, catering to those seeking deeper insights into their practice. Many reviewers appreciate the peace and positive energy of the space, describing it as a second home. However, there have been concerns regarding the customer service experience, highlighting a few instances of less-than-friendly interactions at the front desk, which could detract from the overall welcoming environment. Despite these criticisms, the studio remains a popular choice among yoga enthusiasts looking to enhance their practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Instructors are knowledgeable and passionate about yoga.
- The studio has a welcoming and peaceful atmosphere.
- Offers a variety of classes and transformative retreats.
- Great energy and community vibes.
- Well-organized classes suitable for all levels.

## Areas for Improvement

- Customer service experience can be unfriendly.
- Issues with price transparency concerning class fees.
- Entrance can be difficult to find, especially at night.
