# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio provides a welcoming and supportive environment for practitioners. The instructors, particularly Riana, are known for their attentiveness and detailed guidance, effectively focusing on posture and alignment. The studio maintains a clean and cozy atmosphere, making it an ideal place to deepen one's knowledge and practice of yoga. Students appreciate the thorough explanations and personalized tips during classes, enhancing their understanding of asanas and proper techniques. Special classes, although priced higher, are regarded as valuable learning opportunities, especially for those with specific conditions like scoliosis. Overall, the studio is committed to fostering a positive learning experience in yoga.
