Ikigai Yoga is a top-rated yoga studio in Central Hong Kong known for its diverse classes and welcoming community.

**Address:** 7th Floor, On Lan Centre, 11-15 On Lan St, Central, Hong Kong

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.9 based on 114 reviews

**Website:** http://www.ikigai.hk/

**Phone:** +852 9013 3701

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming environment for practitioners of all levels, emphasizing mindfulness and personal growth through a diverse range of classes including Deep Stretching, Yin Yoga, Vinyasa, and Ashtanga. The instructors are highly experienced, attentive, and create a nurturing space for students, allowing individuals to explore their yoga practice while catering to varying skill levels. The studio is clean, well-organized, and features small class sizes, promoting personal interaction with teachers and a strong sense of community. Many reviews highlight the accessibility of class bookings through the Mindbody app and the lack of contractual obligations, making it convenient for regular practitioners. The overall atmosphere is described as calming and supportive, perfect for those seeking relaxation or a challenge in their yoga practice. Additionally, the studio includes great facilities and encourages camaraderie among its members.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and attentive instructors
- Clean and well-maintained facilities
- Diverse range of classes for all levels
- Strong sense of community and welcoming atmosphere
- Easy booking system without contracts

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes may not align with typical office worker schedules
- Limited natural light due to small windows
- Some students find the space less open and fresh
