A well-regarded yoga studio in Starstreet Precinct, Hong Kong, known for its quality instructors but marred by poor service.

**Address:** 3-19 Wing Fung St, Starstreet Precinct, Hong Kong

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 3.9 based on 53 reviews

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a mixed experience with a generally cozy atmosphere and world-class yoga instructors. Many patrons appreciate the unique ambiance and the quality of instruction, particularly praising specific instructors for their expertise. However, there are significant complaints regarding customer service, especially at the reception desk, where staff tend to be unhelpful or rude. Additionally, issues with mobile phone usage and loud conversations disrupt the peaceful environment expected in a yoga studio. While the café adds to the appeal, its limited food options could use improvement to enhance the overall experience.

Overall, the studio attracts a dedicated clientele who find value in the yoga offered despite the shortcomings in service.

## Why You'll Love It

- World-class and highly qualified yoga instructors.
- Cozy atmosphere with enough shower facilities.
- Unique studio environment that many find appealing.

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor customer service and unhelpful reception staff.
- Issues with mobile phone usage and loud conversations.
- Limited food options at the café.
