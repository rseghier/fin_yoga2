# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of classes, catering to both beginners and seasoned practitioners. With knowledgeable instructors like Goran, Anna, and Andrea, students appreciate personalized attention and clear guidance during sessions. The welcoming atmosphere fosters a sense of community, making it easy for newcomers to feel at home. The studio is bright and clean, with amenities like mat rental included in class passes. Various styles are available, including hot yoga, reformer Pilates, and barre, all delivered with a focus on challenge and relaxation. However, some customers have expressed concerns regarding the studio's customer service, particularly related to communication issues and billing discrepancies.

Overall, this studio is highly recommended for its excellent teaching staff and supportive environment.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly qualified and friendly instructors
- Diverse range of classes for all skill levels
- Welcoming and supportive atmosphere
- Clean and well-maintained facilities
- Value-added services like mat rental included

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with communication and customer service regarding billing
- Delayed response times from staff on membership inquiries
- Some class schedules can be difficult to book
