# Studio Presentation

This studio offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, creating an ideal space for yoga practice. The instructor, Vicky, is praised for her calming and inclusive teaching style, catering to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced yogis. The beautiful location overlooking Muriwai beach adds to the experience, allowing participants to connect with nature during their practice. Classes incorporate thoughtful elements like sound bowls and guided meditation, leaving attendees feeling rejuvenated, calm, and spiritually uplifted. The sauna available after class is a unique feature that enhances relaxation and self-care post-yoga. Overall, the studio fosters a sense of community among participants and is highly recommended by attendees for its beautiful environment and skilled instructors.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and welcoming atmosphere
- Excellent and calming teaching style by Vicky
- Beautiful location overlooking Muriwai beach
- Community-oriented environment with local participants
- Unique finishing touches like sauna and sound bowl meditation
