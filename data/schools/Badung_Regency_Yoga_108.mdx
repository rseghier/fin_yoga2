# Studio Presentation

Located in the serene surroundings of Badung Regency, this yoga studio offers a warm and inviting atmosphere for practitioners of all levels. With skilled teachers who are praised for their kindness and humor, students can enjoy a variety of classes that challenge both the mind and body. The studio emphasizes a no-judgment policy, making it a perfect space for beginners and seasoned yogis alike. Many reviews highlight the incredible energy within the studio and the supportive community that welcomes everyone, regardless of their experience. The affordable class rates and the flexibility of walk-in sessions add to the accessibility of the studio, making it a favorite spot for travelers seeking wellness during their stay in Bali. However, some visitors noted concerns about crowded classes and ventilation issues.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and inviting atmosphere with skilled and kind teachers.
- Challenging and well-structured classes suitable for all levels.
- Affordable pricing with flexible options for walk-in sessions.
- Supportive and welcoming community for yogis of all backgrounds.
- Incredible energy in the studio enhances the practice experience.

## Areas for Improvement

- Class sizes can be crowded, leading to less personal space.
- Ventilation issues reported, causing discomfort during hot classes.
- Payment is cash-only, which may inconvenience some visitors.
- Some instructors' teaching styles may not resonate with all students.
- Lack of amenities such as showers or a proper chill space post-class.
