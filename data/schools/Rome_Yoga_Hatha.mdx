# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming and comfortable environment where participants can enjoy Hatha yoga classes tailored to their skill levels. With a maximum capacity of eight students, the intimate setting allows for personalized attention from the instructor. The presence of music during sessions enhances the overall experience, making each class dynamic and engaging.
