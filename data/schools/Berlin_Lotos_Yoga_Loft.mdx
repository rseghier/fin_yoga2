# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming atmosphere and a beautiful space designed for yoga practice. It is appreciated for its healthy back classes, which have received positive feedback from participants. The studio has the advantage of experienced teachers who contribute to the overall positive experience of the students. However, some reviews mention a lack of basic elements like om chanting and pranayama, and there were concerns regarding the instructor's engagement with students during classes. Overall, it seems to be a good choice for practitioners, especially in the Friedrichshain area.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming atmosphere
- Beautiful space
- Experienced teachers
- Popular healthy back classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Lack of engagement from the instructor during classes
- No basic practices like om chanting or pranayama
- Limited adjustments made to students
