# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Berlin offers a unique blend of Jivamukti yoga in a spacious and beautifully designed environment. It is well-known for its exceptional instructors, each bringing their own approach to teaching. The studio hosts a variety of classes, including Open classes which cater to yogis with prior experience. However, some reviews indicate inconsistencies in class quality, particularly regarding loud music and teaching styles that may not suit everyone's needs. The facility is praised for its airy space and engaging atmosphere, promoting both skill development and relaxation. While the teachers receive high marks for professionalism, customer service experiences vary, with some patrons expressing disappointment over staffing and operational practices. Overall, it remains a favorite among many local practitioners seeking a supportive and inspiring yoga environment.

## Why You'll Love It

- Spacious and beautifully designed environment
- Incredible, knowledgeable yoga instructors
- Variety of class offerings including advanced options
- Supportive and engaging atmosphere for practice
- Included amenities such as video streams and live sessions

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistent class quality; some reviews mention distracting music
- Customer service can be perceived as rude or unhelpful
- Lack of enclosed changing areas
- Difficulties accessing policies and contract details
- Instances of uncomfortable interactions between staff and customers
