# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Coyoacán is highly recommended for both beginners and experienced practitioners. Reviewers highlight the professionalism and warmth of the instructors, who are dedicated to sharing their knowledge and helping students grow personally. The studio fosters a welcoming community, making each class a unique and special experience. However, customers have noted issues with customer service, particularly regarding phone inquiries and the rigidity of package limitations. Classes offered are diverse, including popular options like Aeroyoga and Hatha Vinyasa, although some classes may start late or have unusual formats. Overall, the clean and friendly environment contributes to a positive learning atmosphere.



## Why You'll Love It

- Professional and knowledgeable instructors
- Welcoming and attentive community
- A variety of yoga classes offered, including Aeroyoga and Hatha Vinyasa
- Clean facilities with a good atmosphere
- Support for personal growth through yoga

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor customer service, especially over the phone
- Classes can start late and have unusual formats
- Unrealistic limitations on package purchases
- Inconsistent communication regarding class details
- Some competition with other nearby studios
