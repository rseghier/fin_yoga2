The Yoga Space is a hidden gem located in Balaclava, Melbourne, offering a peaceful atmosphere and knowledgeable instructors.

**Address:** Ground Floor/25 William St, Balaclava VIC 3183, Australia

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.9 based on 47 reviews

**Website:** https://www.layawellness.com.au/bookings/yoga-space

**Phone:** +61 477 888 678

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a spacious and beautifully designed environment dedicated entirely to yoga, making it comfortable for practitioners to stretch and practice without feeling cramped. The instructors are highly praised for their knowledge, compassion, and ability to create unique and thoughtful classes that cater to different needs. Regular classes, especially yin yoga, are highlighted as a participant favorite, providing a tranquil and rejuvenating experience. However, some classes have been noted for a repetitive flow, and communication regarding scheduling and events could be improved. Despite a few quirks, the overall vibe is warm and inviting, making it a supportive community for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Spacious and beautifully designed studio dedicated to yoga.
- Highly knowledgeable and compassionate teachers.
- Strong sense of community among participants.
- Excellent yin yoga classes that are very popular.
- Calm atmosphere with gentle lighting.

## Areas for Improvement

- Repetitive class flows in some evening sessions.
- Poor communication regarding class cancellations and events.
- Unintuitive online booking system.
- Pricing structure may be confusing.
- Inconsistent updates on social media about giveaways and events.
