# Studio Presentation

This studio offers a diverse range of classes including Zumba, Pilates, infrared yoga, and aerial yoga, catering to various fitness preferences. The instructors, such as Ms. Melody Poo, are noted for their ability to make classes enjoyable and accessible, especially for beginners. The atmosphere is generally positive and energetic, particularly during Zumba sessions where music and excitement play a key role. However, some clients have expressed dissatisfaction with communication regarding class packages, highlighting a lack of outreach from management. Concerns about instructorsâ€™ qualifications were also raised, suggesting that some less experienced participants might be at risk. Despite these concerns, pricing for classes is considered fair and flexible, with an easy online booking system, though some logistical issues were noted regarding card requirements for entry.

## Why You'll Love It

- Diverse range of classes offered
- Energetic and fun Zumba sessions led by a skilled instructor
- Fair and flexible pricing options
- Simple online booking system

## Areas for Improvement

- Lack of communication regarding class packages
- Concerns about instructor qualifications, especially for beginners
- Troublesome requirement to bring a physical card for class entry
