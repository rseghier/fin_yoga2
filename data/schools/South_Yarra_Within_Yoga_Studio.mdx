# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is a welcoming sanctuary located in South Yarra, Victoria, where practitioners of all levels can find a nurturing environment to grow in their yoga journey. The studio boasts a friendly and supportive community, composed of skilled instructors who offer a variety of classes. With a focus on both challenging and gentle yoga styles, the studio caters to beginners and advanced yogis alike. The atmosphere is described as cozy, peaceful, and clean, enhancing the overall experience. However, there have been some concerns regarding space management during classes and the need for greater focus on safe alignment instructions. Despite these issues, the majority of reviews reflect a deep appreciation for the instructors and the overall vibe of the studio.

## Why You'll Love It

- Incredible and skilled instructors who cater to all levels of experience.
- Friendly and supportive community atmosphere.
- Cozy, peaceful, and clean environment.
- Diverse class offerings that include challenging and gentle styles.
- Strong sense of safety and comfort for students.

## Areas for Improvement

- Concerns about space management during dynamic classes.
- Issues with instructors not providing alignment assistance.
- Some classes felt more like a gymnastic session than a spiritual practice.
- Occasional discomfort noted regarding gender inclusivity.
