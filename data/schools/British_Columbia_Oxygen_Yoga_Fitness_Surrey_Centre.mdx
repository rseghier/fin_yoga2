# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Surrey offers a welcoming and family-like atmosphere that is enhanced by its clean facilities and attentive management. Many reviews highlight the friendly interaction among staff and students, making it a comfortable place to work out. The variety of classes caters to all fitness levels, ensuring that everyone can find a suitable option for their needs. Instructors are often praised for their dedication and ability to connect with students, providing modifications tailored to individual capabilities. However, some reviews point out significant issues with customer service, especially regarding handling of late arrivals, which has negatively affected the overall experience for a few clients. Despite these concerns, the studio is generally regarded as one of the best in the area for hot yoga and fitness.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and friendly atmosphere
- Clean and spacious studio
- Dedicated and encouraging instructors
- Variety of classes for all fitness levels
- Strong community feeling among attendees

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor customer service, particularly with late arrivals
- Management tends to prioritize profits over service quality
