# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio specializes in Ashtanga yoga, offering a welcoming environment for practitioners of all levels. Instructors are highly knowledgeable and dedicated, providing personalized guidance suitable for both beginners and seasoned yogis. The studio frequently organizes courses and retreats, contributing to a richer understanding of yoga beyond physical practice.
A focus on professional and authentic Ashtanga yoga, rooted in the tradition of Sri K. Pattabhi Jois, allows students to progress at their own pace, fostering a sense of community and belonging. The atmosphere is described as peaceful and nurturing, making it an ideal space for personal growth and deep exploration of yoga philosophy.
Located in a well-connected area of Milan, the studio ensures ease of access for all practitioners, with convenient parking available. Overall, this studio is regarded as a unique refuge in the bustling city, emphasizing both the physical and philosophical aspects of yoga.
