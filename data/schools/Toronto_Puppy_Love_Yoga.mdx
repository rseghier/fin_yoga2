Puppy Love Yoga is a unique yoga studio located in Toronto that combines yoga with the joy of playing with puppies.

**Address:** 243 Coxwell Ave, Toronto, ON M4L 3B4, Canada

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4 based on 49 reviews

**Website:** https://puppyloveyoga.com/

**Phone:** None

# Studio Presentation

This unique yoga studio combines the joy of yoga with the love of puppies, creating an unforgettable and calming experience. The instructors are noted for their attentiveness and friendly demeanor, making it an inclusive environment suitable for beginners. While guests enjoy yoga, they also have the opportunity to interact with adorable puppies, providing pure bliss at the end of each session. The studio space is clean, and parking is convenient, further enhancing the experience. However, some concerns have been raised regarding the treatment of the puppies and the professionalism of the management, which may affect overall satisfaction. Despite these issues, the friendly staff and the delightful puppy experience continue to attract visitors seeking a stress-relieving activity.



## Why You'll Love It

- Amazing and attentive yoga instructors.
- Welcoming and friendly environment.
- Unique experience combining yoga with puppies.
- Clean space with easy parking access.
- Suitable for beginners.

## Areas for Improvement

- Concerns regarding the welfare of very young puppies.
- Management reported as unprofessional and aggressive.
- No secure storage for personal belongings.
- Difficult to find due to the old building.
- Mixed experiences with customer service.
