# Studio Presentation

This studio offers a tranquil oasis within Kuala Lumpur, surrounded by lush greenery that enhances the calming atmosphere for yoga practice. Patrons rave about the spacious classrooms, excellent ventilation, and the relaxing environment that includes the soothing sounds of nature. With a variety of classes available, from Yin Yoga to Sound Healing, students appreciate the knowledgeable and encouraging instructors who cater to all skill levels. The studio is also adjacent to a vegetarian café, providing healthy food options, although some have noted the café could improve its pricing and quality. Overall, this studio is highly recommended for both beginners and seasoned yogis looking for a peaceful retreat in the heart of the city.



## Why You'll Love It

- Calming and serene atmosphere with lush greenery.
- Spacious classrooms with excellent ventilation.
- Knowledgeable and encouraging instructors.
- Variety of classes offered for different skill levels.
- Friendly and welcoming staff.

## Areas for Improvement

- Café adjacent to studio is overpriced and quality could improve.
- Some reviews mention a receptionist is not always smiley.
