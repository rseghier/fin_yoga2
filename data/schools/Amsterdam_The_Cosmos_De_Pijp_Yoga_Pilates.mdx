The Cosmos de Pijp is a beautiful yoga and pilates studio located in Amsterdam.

**Address:** Lutmastraat 191D, 1074 TV Amsterdam, Netherlands

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.1 based on 13 reviews

**Website:** https://the-cosmos.nl/

**Phone:** None

# Studio Presentation

This yoga and pilates studio is recognized for its beautiful and calming space, featuring great facilities and a clean environment. The instructors, especially Andreas, are highly praised for their exceptional teaching skills, particularly in yin yoga. The atmosphere is described as inviting, with good energy from both the staff and fellow participants, creating a supportive community. However, some classes may be fast-paced depending on the instructor. There have been some concerns regarding management practices related to ClassPass, which could affect the overall experience for some members.

## Why You'll Love It

- Exceptional instructors, especially for yin yoga and pilates.
- Beautiful and calming space with great facilities.
- Clean environment with individual towels and cleaning supplies.
- Inviting atmosphere with positive energy.
- Supportive community vibe among participants.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some classes are fast-paced and may not suit everyone's preference.
- Management issues related to ClassPass could lead to confusion and frustration.
- Limited options for more challenging pilates classes.
