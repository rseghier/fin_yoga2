# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a unique and enriching experience in a boutique setting in Kyoto. The instructor is highly regarded for her expertise in anatomy and exercise physiology, making classes adaptable to various skill levels. Personalized attention is a hallmark of the practice, allowing students to experience therapeutic yoga that addresses individual needs. Classes often incorporate props, sound healing methods such as sound bowls, and are conducted in both English and Japanese, catering to a diverse clientele. Additionally, the studio hosts intimate retreats in beautiful settings, enhancing the overall yoga experience with cultural elements and local insights. The welcoming atmosphere fosters a sense of community, making it an excellent choice for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.
