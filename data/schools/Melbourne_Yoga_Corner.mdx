# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is a welcoming sanctuary located in the bustling Melbourne CBD, offering a variety of classes that cater to all levels of practitioners. With a focus on quality teaching, the instructors are described as knowledgeable, friendly, and supportive, enhancing both personal growth and professional development. The studio features a comfortable vibe with access to props and amenities like mats, blocks, and even blankets for added warmth. Many reviews highlight transformational teacher training programs, particularly praising the trainers for their deep understanding and passionate approaches to yoga. However, there are some concerns regarding incense use and the booking system. Overall, it is a place that embodies the essence of yoga and encourages individuals to grow both on and off the mat.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and friendly atmosphere
- Knowledgeable and experienced instructors
- Diverse range of classes available
- Focus on both personal and professional growth
- Comfortable studio environment with necessary props

## Areas for Improvement

- Incense use may cause discomfort for some students
- Clunky and erroneous booking system
- Some pricing concerns after signing up for a membership
- Loss of personal items reported by a customer
