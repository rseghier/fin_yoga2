# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio, previously operational in Goa, India, was known for its beautiful beachfront location and strong community atmosphere. It offered a variety of yoga classes, including Ashtanga, Vinyasa, and Yin styles, catering to practitioners of all levels. The studio had a reputation for its friendly and supportive staff, excellent food, and numerous opportunities for teaching and volunteering. However, it faced severe issues leading to its permanent closure, including legal troubles that impacted its operations and financial dealings. Many reviews noted the disappointment regarding the lack of communication about the studio's status and difficulties in obtaining refunds. Despite the positive experiences of some guests, the closure has left a negative impression on the wider yoga community.
