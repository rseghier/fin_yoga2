# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and beautiful location right next to the Neel Ganga River, providing a peaceful atmosphere ideal for yoga practice. 
It serves as a center for research and training in yoga, emphasizing the importance of both physical and spiritual growth. 
Additionally, the studio is involved in community service through its Anna Kshetra program, offering a free meal each day to those in need. 
With its dedication to both yoga and community, it embodies the principles of compassion and selfless service.
