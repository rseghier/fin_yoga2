# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and beautiful environment, nestled among nature with stunning views of the ocean and jungle. The atmosphere is peaceful and energizing, enriched by the sounds of nature, including monkeys and birds. With a range of drop-in classes available, participants have the opportunity to experience various styles, primarily vinyasa and hatha, providing options for different skill levels. Teachers are noted for their knowledge, warmth, and ability to create a supportive experience. Classes include a complimentary breakfast, adding to the overall positive experience. Visitors have remarked on the community feel, though some noted an overwhelming number of vinyasa classes as a minor downside. Overall, it is highly recommended for both new and experienced yogis seeking a memorable practice amidst nature.

## Why You'll Love It

- Stunning natural setting with views of the ocean and jungle
- Knowledgeable and supportive teachers
- Complimentary healthy breakfast included with classes
- Variety of class options for different skill levels
- Positive community atmosphere

## Areas for Improvement

- A predominance of vinyasa classes over other styles
- Some reviews indicate a cliquey atmosphere for solo travelers
- Hatha classes have been described as less traditional than expected
