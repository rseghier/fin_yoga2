# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Subang Jaya offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, catering to practitioners of all levels. The experienced instructor is known for her engaging classes, where she balances fun and intensity beautifully. Students appreciate her keen attention to detail and her ability to provide variations of poses for both beginners and advanced participants. The studio is clean, well-ventilated, and spacious, contributing to a serene practice environment. Reviews highlight the supportive community and the challenging nature of the classes, especially in Ashtanga yoga. However, some concerns have been raised regarding privacy during classes and the need for better management of class sizes to enhance the overall experience. Overall, this studio has created a cheerful vibe that encourages ongoing improvement and a love for yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and engaging instructor known for attention to detail.
- Supportive community that fosters improvement and enjoyment in practice.
- Clean, well-ventilated, and spacious studio environment.
- Variety of classes available catering to different skill levels.
- Challenging yet fun classes that encourage personal growth.

## Areas for Improvement

- Invasive behavior of staff taking photos/videos during classes.
- Crowded classes that may overwhelm the available space.
- Class language inconsistency caused confusion for non-Mandarin speakers.
