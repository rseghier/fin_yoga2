# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is located in Newtown, New South Wales, and is recognized for its vibrant atmosphere and supportive community. Many reviews highlight the strong focus on traditional practices, with teachers who provide hands-on adjustments and foster an inclusive environment. However, there's also mention of issues with class sizes, which can feel cramped in smaller rooms. The studio has a diverse range of classes that cater to various levels, although some feedback suggests that certain classes can be repetitive. Overall, it presents itself as a welcoming space for yoga enthusiasts looking to deepen their practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Vibrant atmosphere with great vibes
- Knowledgeable and supportive teachers
- Focus on traditional yoga practices
- Hands-on adjustments during classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes can feel cramped due to high attendance
- Some classes have repetitive movements
- Issues related to the management and teacher retention
