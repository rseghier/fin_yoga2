Puppy Yoga in Toronto offers a fun blend of yoga and puppy interactions, but suffers from significant customer service issues.

**Address:** 213 Sterling Rd, Toronto, ON M6R 2B2, Canada

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 2.9 based on 75 reviews

**Website:** http://www.puppyyoga.com/

**Phone:** None

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a unique blend of yoga practice and playful interaction with puppies. Many reviews commend the joy and relaxation that come from participating in classes filled with adorable puppies, describing the experience as a fantastic way to disconnect from daily stress. However, there are significant operational issues, such as frequent class cancellations, poor customer service, and difficulty with refunds. Customers have reported frustrating communication barriers, including unanswered emails and changes to class schedules without proper notice. Despite the delightful atmosphere created by the puppies, there are numerous complaints regarding the management's responsiveness and professionalism, detracting from the overall experience.

Overall, customers express a strong desire for improvements in communication and organization, indicating that enhancements in service could elevate an otherwise charmed environment into a trusted and enjoyable venture.

## Why You'll Love It

- Unique and enjoyable experience combining yoga with puppy play.
- Positive atmosphere that brings joy and relaxation to participants.
- Great for families, appealing to both children and adults.

## Areas for Improvement

- Numerous class cancellations and poor handling of bookings.
- Lack of responsive customer service and communication.
- Difficulty locating the venue due to inadequate signage and instructions.
