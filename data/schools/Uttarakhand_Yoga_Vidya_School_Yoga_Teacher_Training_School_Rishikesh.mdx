# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a variety of training options, including online and in-person courses, focusing on holistic practices that encompass physical postures, breathing techniques, and yogic philosophies. The instructors are described as knowledgeable and attentive, with some students highlighting transformational experiences in their mental and emotional well-being. However, there are significant concerns regarding the organization and quality of courses. Many reviews note a lack of timely feedback, poor translation quality for non-native speakers, and inconsistent teaching methodologies. Despite these weaknesses, students appreciate skilled guest teachers and the community atmosphere that fosters personal growth.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and attentive instructors
- Transformational experiences in mental and emotional well-being
- Variety of yoga courses available, including online options
- Good guest teachers enhancing learning experiences

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor organization and lack of timely feedback
- Inadequate quality of translation for non-native speakers
- Inconsistent teaching methodologies and classroom management
- Changes in training schedules after payment made
