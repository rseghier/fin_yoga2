RYOGA, located in Trastevere, Rome, is a well-regarded yoga studio known for its professional instructors and variety of classes.

**Address:** Via dei Fienaroli, 28, 00153 Roma RM, Italy

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.7 based on 234 reviews

**Website:** https://www.ryoga.com/

**Phone:** +39 06 580 6498

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a wide variety of classes suitable for all levels, taught by skilled and passionate teachers. The environment is described as relaxing and welcoming, making it a great place for individuals to find balance in their physical and mental health. Many reviews highlight the professionalism and kindness of the staff, from the reception to the instructors. The studio is located in a beautiful and historic area, adding to its charm. However, some reviews note issues such as overcrowded classes and cleanliness concerns, specifically regarding air circulation and maintenance of the facilities. Despite these weaknesses, the positive atmosphere and quality of instruction have led many to recommend this studio as one of the best facilities for yoga practice in Rome.

## Why You'll Love It

- Wide variety of yoga classes for different skill levels
- Highly qualified and passionate instructors
- Welcoming and relaxing atmosphere
- Convenient location in a beautiful area
- Friendly and professional front desk staff

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes can be overcrowded, making reservation difficult
- Issues with cleanliness and maintenance in the facilities
- Poor air circulation in the yoga rooms
- Rigid administrative organization regarding membership options
- Some classes may lack traditional yoga practices
