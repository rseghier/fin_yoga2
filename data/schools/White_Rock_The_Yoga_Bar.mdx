# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of classes, including Hot Yoga, Aerial, Barre, Circuit Training, and Deep Stretch, catering to various fitness levels. The instructors are frequently praised for their professionalism, dedication, and ability to provide modifications for different skill levels, contributing to a welcoming and supportive atmosphere. The studio is designed with a clean and inviting space, featuring amenities such as showers and locker rooms. Many clients appreciate the ocean view that enhances their practice experience. However, recent reviews highlight severe issues with transparency and customer service, particularly regarding the sudden closure of locations and unaddressed refund requests, which have left many clients frustrated. Despite its strengths in class variety and instructor quality, concerns about management practices and studio maintenance have emerged.

## Why You'll Love It

- Diverse range of classes including Hot Yoga, Aerial, and Circuit Training.
- Knowledgeable and professional instructors providing excellent guidance.
- Clean, inviting environment with good amenities and ocean view.

## Areas for Improvement

- Sudden closure of multiple locations without proper notification.
- Poor communication regarding refunds and customer service.
- Maintenance issues with studio facilities not being addressed.
