# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a beautiful and spacious environment for both hot yoga and Pilates, complete with outdoor showers and a bamboo garden. Many reviews praise the welcoming atmosphere, high-quality teachers, and transformative classes that emphasize mindfulness and breath awareness. However, some long-time members have expressed dissatisfaction with recent changes in class management and the overall vibe, noting a decline in the welcoming atmosphere and community feel. Technical issues with booking and unfriendly staff encounters have also been reported, causing frustration among members. Despite these challenges, the studio still attracts many who leave feeling energized and uplifted after their sessions.

## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful, spacious studio with outdoor amenities
- High-quality and supportive teachers
- Transformative classes focused on mindfulness
- Welcoming community atmosphere
- Good facilities including free mat use

## Areas for Improvement

- Recent decline in the quality of service and atmosphere
- Booking and app issues causing difficulties for members
- Experiences of unfriendly staff and rude encounters
- Classes can be overcrowded and chaotic
- Changes in class structure that some feel detract from quality
