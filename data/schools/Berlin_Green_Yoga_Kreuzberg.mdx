# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is a hidden gem in the heart of Kreuzberg, Berlin, offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere for practitioners of all levels. The studio is known for its community spirit, with friendly teachers who are committed to making each class a unique experience. Many reviewers praised the variety of yoga styles available, from vigorous sessions to calming Yin Yoga with gongs. While some classes can run over time, the engaging and supportive instructors help create a safe space for yoga practice. However, some logistical issues, such as missed online classes and time management problems, have been highlighted by attendees. Overall, it provides a charming escape from the chaotic city life of Berlin, making it an inviting place for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and friendly community atmosphere
- Variety of yoga styles available
- Engaging and attentive teachers
- Unique features like Gong Yoga
- Cozy space with good amenities

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistent time management in classes
- Occasional missed online sessions without notice
- Some teachers may not respect personal boundaries
- Logistical issues like access problems for attendees
- Sick teachers putting students at risk
