# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is an elegant and calming space located in the center of Lagny-sur-Marne. It offers a cozy atmosphere filled with positive energy, making it an ideal spot for various yoga practices, including Yoga, Pilates, and Garuda. The owner, Sophie, is described as attentive and compassionate, ensuring that every student feels welcomed and supported. The studio provides necessary equipment and maintains a clean environment, highlighting the professionalism of the teaching staff. With classes tailored to individual needs and a strong sense of community, participants consistently report feelings of relaxation and well-being after sessions. Overall, this studio is a haven of peace that promotes inclusivity and mindfulness in every practice.
