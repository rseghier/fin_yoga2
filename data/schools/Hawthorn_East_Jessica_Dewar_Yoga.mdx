# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a small and cozy environment, equipped with all necessary tools such as mats, blocks, and straps for a comfortable practice.
The instructor, Ilaria, is noted for her knowledge and authentic energy, providing a well-structured flow during classes. 
Despite the positive experience shared by some customers, there are concerns about accessibility, as attempts to reach the studio via phone and email have been unsuccessful.
Overall, it is a welcoming space for those seeking a personal and grounded yoga experience.
