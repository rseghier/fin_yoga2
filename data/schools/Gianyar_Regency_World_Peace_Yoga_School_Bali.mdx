# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a transformative experience deeply rooted in traditional yogic teachings. With an emphasis on knowledge and community, students report incredible engagement from knowledgeable teachers who bring unique perspectives, enriching the learning process. The curriculum covers diverse styles such as Ashtanga, Hatha, and Kundalini, alongside philosophy and anatomy. Attendees highlight the nourishing vegetarian food and comfortable accommodations as significant perks. Nested in the serene environment of Ubud, this studio promotes a holistic approach to yoga, focusing not only on physical practice but also on mental and spiritual growth. The supportive atmosphere encourages personal development and fosters strong connections among students. Many note that their time here feels like being part of a loving family, filled with laughter, learning, and shared experiences. Overall, it is recommended for anyone seeking an authentic and impactful yoga journey.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and passionate teachers
- Transformative and immersive training experience
- Supportive and friendly community atmosphere
- High-quality vegetarian food
- Comfortable accommodations

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistencies between advertised courses and actual offerings
- Cleanliness issues with accommodations and yoga mats
- Scheduling challenges with days off and class modifications
- Occasional lack of clarity in communication from staff
- Repetitive meals lacking variety
