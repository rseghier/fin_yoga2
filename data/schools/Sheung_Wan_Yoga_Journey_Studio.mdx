# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming atmosphere and is noted for its cleanliness and peace. Customers appreciate the supportive community and highlight the skills of one of the instructors, Flora. However, there are significant concerns regarding professionalism, particularly related to class bookings and instructor attendance. These issues detract from an otherwise positive experience at the studio.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming crowd
- Very clean and peaceful environment
- Skilled instructor, Flora

## Areas for Improvement

- Unprofessional handling of class bookings
- Instructor did not arrive for a scheduled class
- Loss of funds due to booking issues
