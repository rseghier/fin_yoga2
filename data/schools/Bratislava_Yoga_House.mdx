Yoga House, located in Bratislava, is a welcoming and clean yoga studio known for its attentive instructors.

**Address:** Panenská 682, 811 03 Bratislava, Slovakia

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.7 based on 52 reviews

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a pleasant and inviting atmosphere with clean, bright spaces. The instructors are highly regarded for their attentiveness, helping students with posture adjustments and providing alternative exercises. Many reviews highlight the kindness and friendliness of the staff, contributing to a warm community feel. However, the capacity of the classes has been criticized, with some reports of overcrowding making it difficult to practice comfortably. Class reservations can easily be arranged online, and there are some complimentary snacks available. Overall, it’s a wonderful place for yoga enthusiasts, though it could benefit from reevaluating class sizes to enhance the experience.



## Why You'll Love It

- Clean and bright facilities
- Kind and attentive instructors
- Warm and friendly staff
- Wide variety of yoga courses offered
- Complimentary snacks available

## Areas for Improvement

- Overcrowded classes make it hard to relax
- Insufficient space in the changing rooms
- Limited room for movement during sessions
