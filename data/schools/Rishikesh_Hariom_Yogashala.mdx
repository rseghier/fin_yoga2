# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comprehensive training program that includes asana, pranayama, and shatkarma, along with teachings in Ayurveda, philosophy, and meditation. The instructors are highly praised, known for their excellent teaching skills and deep knowledge of yoga. A strong emphasis on personal attention ensures students receive guidance on alignments during classes. The studio features stunning views of the Ganga River, enhancing the overall experience. Many reviews highlight the nurturing and supportive atmosphere, contributing to unforgettable experiences for students. The studio has successfully attracted both long-term students and those interested in drop-in classes, affirming its welcoming nature and commitment to yoga education.
