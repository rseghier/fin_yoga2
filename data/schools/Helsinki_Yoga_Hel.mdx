# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, making it a popular choice for practitioners in Kallio. Known for being the second largest hot yoga studio in Helsinki, it features a modern building and a variety of hot classes and workshops led by professional teachers. Many reviews applaud the dedicated staff and the overall quality of the facilities. It is particularly well-regarded by those who appreciate bikram yoga. However, there is a call for more class options, especially afternoon sessions, to accommodate the schedules of local practitioners.

Overall, this studio balances size and comfort, providing a great yoga experience in a vibrant community setting.

## Why You'll Love It

- Cozy atmosphere
- Modern building
- Variety of hot classes and workshops
- Professional teachers
- Welcoming and dedicated staff

## Areas for Improvement

- Need for more class options
- Lack of afternoon classes
