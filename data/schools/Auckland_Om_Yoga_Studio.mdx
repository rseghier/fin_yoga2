# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a calming and supportive environment, ideal for beginners and experienced practitioners alike. With a range of classes including Yin Yang Yoga, Vinyasa Flows, and Yoga Nidra, it caters to diverse needs and skill levels. Instructors are known for their friendly, helpful nature, guiding students through various poses with personalized variations. The spacious and light-filled studio includes amenities such as lockers and showers for added convenience. However, the studio has a strict policy on late arrivals, which has disappointed some clients. Overall, it's a great place to start your yoga journey and experience positive changes in your life.



## Why You'll Love It

- Calming and supportive environment
- Friendly and helpful instructors
- Variety of classes available for different skill levels
- Spacious and light-filled studio
- Amenities like lockers and showers

## Areas for Improvement

- Strict late arrival policy, locking out latecomers
- Best hot flow class only available during working hours
- Lack of body modifications in classes
