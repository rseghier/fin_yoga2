# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a blend of knowledgeable and dedicated instructors, creating a supportive and nurturing environment for yoga practice. The facilities are described as beautiful and well-maintained, with a warm atmosphere especially appreciated during the winter months. However, there are significant concerns raised regarding cleanliness and the flow of classes, with interruptions noted during sessions. Overall, the studio is recommended for its strong focus on ashtanga yoga, including options for online classes.

The positive feedback highlights the inviting ambiance and the quality of teaching, which seems to resonate well with many practitioners. Yet, the severe criticisms regarding overcrowding and hygiene cannot be overlooked, affecting the overall experience for some attendees.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and dedicated instructors
- Beautiful and warm facilities
- Strong focus on ashtanga yoga
- Supportive and nurturing environment

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with cleanliness
- Classes often interrupted by people entering and exiting
- Overcrowding reported
