# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is situated within the serene environment of the Swami Narayan Mandir in Powai, Mumbai. The classes are conducted in the morning and evening, making it accessible for various schedules. With a focus on traditional yoga, the studio emphasizes the balance of mental and physical well-being, incorporating not only asanas but also meditation techniques and spiritual lectures. The atmosphere is described as calming and divine, enhancing the overall experience. The studio is run by highly qualified instructors, led by Dr. Deepak Parmar, a PhD in Yoga with expertise in stress management. The commitment to teaching and preserving traditional methods underscores its reputation as an excellent place for all ages seeking genuine yoga practice.

Future development plans include constructing a state-of-the-art International Yoga Centre, promising enhancement to the already vibrant yoga community.
