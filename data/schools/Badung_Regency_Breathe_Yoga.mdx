# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere with a strong emphasis on personalized instruction. The small class sizes allow for individual attention, making it suitable for practitioners of various skill levels, including beginners. Reviewers frequently highlight the attentiveness and kindness of the instructors, who provide modifications to accommodate different abilities. The studio is known for its affordable pricing and natural environment, situated amidst the serene surroundings of Bali. Some classes, such as Aerial Yoga and Vinyasa, have been particularly praised for their comprehensive structure and the calming ambiance created by nature. However, reviews also suggest a few concerns regarding cleanliness, specifically with occasional natural disturbances like caterpillars. Overall, this studio stands out for its authentic local experience and dedicated teaching staff.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and attentive instructors who cater to different skill levels
- Affordable pricing compared to other studios in the area
- Small class sizes for personalized attention
- Natural and peaceful studio environment
- Wide variety of yoga styles offered including Aerial Yoga and Vinyasa

## Areas for Improvement

- Occasional issues with cleanliness due to natural disturbances like caterpillars
- Some reviews indicated discomfort related to these disturbances
