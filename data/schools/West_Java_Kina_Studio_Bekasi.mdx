# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Bekasi offers a variety of classes including core pilates, yoga, pound-fit, and yoga sticks, providing a vibrant atmosphere that promotes enjoyment and fitness. Members appreciate the engaging instructors who create a fun and supportive environment, making workouts feel less strenuous. Participants note positive changes in their physical fitness and body shape, highlighting the effective programs available. However, some reviews mention distractions from noise and issues with restroom facilities, indicating areas for improvement. Despite these minor inconveniences, the overall experience at the studio is favorable, thanks to its dynamic classes and skilled instructors.

## Why You'll Love It

- Engaging instructors who create a fun atmosphere
- Effective classes leading to noticeable fitness results
- Variety of classes available to cater to different preferences

## Areas for Improvement

- Noise distractions during classes
- Restroom facilities need improvement, especially for waiting times
