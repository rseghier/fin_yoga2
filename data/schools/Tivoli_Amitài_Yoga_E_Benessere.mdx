# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and enchanting atmosphere, with two locations: one by a breathtaking waterfall in Tivoli and another in the tranquil countryside of Castel Madama. The instructor, Agnese, is highly appreciated for her kindness and ability to make both beginners and experienced practitioners feel at ease. Each session is designed to be restorative and relaxing, set in elegantly minimalistic spaces that promote introspection. Additionally, the studio organizes wellness events featuring naturopathy, mindfulness, and Ayurveda, fostering a sense of community and personal growth. Overall, it is a perfect refuge for those seeking balance, strength, and inner peace in harmony with nature.
