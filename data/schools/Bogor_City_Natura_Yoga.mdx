# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is located in Bogor City, West Java, and is noted for its serene atmosphere and cleanliness, making it a peaceful place for yoga practice. Many reviews highlight the strategic location and comfortable environment, especially for specialized classes such as couple prenatal yoga. The quality of the instructors receives high praise, indicating that they are knowledgeable and supportive. However, there are significant concerns regarding professionalism, particularly with scheduling and communication. Some clients have reported issues with classes starting on time and delays in responses from staff, which detracts from the overall experience. Despite these weaknesses, the studio remains a popular choice for yoga enthusiasts in the area.



## Why You'll Love It

- Serene and peaceful atmosphere for yoga practice
- Clean and comfortable environment
- Excellent instructors
- Strategic location
- Positive feedback on specialized classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Lack of professionalism in scheduling
- Delayed teacher attendance for reserved classes
- Poor communication from admin regarding class updates
- Slow response times from staff
- Confusion among staff about teacher availability
