# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio features beautiful instruction and a welcoming space. The environment is described as clean, bright, and inviting. There are instructors who are attentive and highly qualified, creating an enriching experience for students. However, some reviews highlight instances of poor customer service, with complaints about how queries regarding subscriptions were handled. Despite this, there are positive mentions of staff members who go out of their way to accommodate students' needs. Overall, the studio offers a mix of fantastic classes with skilled teachers alongside some service-related concerns.



## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful instruction and welcoming space.
- Attentive and highly qualified instructors.
- Clean and bright environment.
- Positive experiences with specific teachers.
- Efforts to accommodate student needs.

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor customer service experiences.
- Unpleasant treatment in communication regarding subscriptions.
- Inconsistent handling of customer queries.
- Concerns about service leading to low attendance.
