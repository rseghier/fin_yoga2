# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Rocklea Queensland offers a variety of classes in a welcoming and relaxing atmosphere. Reviewers highlight the kindness and support of the staff, as well as the spacious and clean environment that invites beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Many customers praise the range of yoga styles taught by dedicated instructors, making it a great place for individuals with diverse fitness needs. However, some clients have expressed concerns regarding communication and membership policies, particularly relating to cancellation issues, which has caused frustration for some members.

Overall, the studio is remembered for its friendly vibe, excellent classes, and beautiful space, making it a recommended choice for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and welcoming atmosphere
- Dedicated and kind staff
- Wide variety of yoga styles and classes
- Clean and spacious facilities
- Great experience for beginners

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor communication regarding membership issues
- Aggressive handling of membership cancellations
- Passive-aggressive behavior reported by some staff
- Concerns about financial practices
