Urban Yoga, located in Kwun Tong, is criticized for its cleanliness and hygiene standards.

**Address:** Rm03, 5/F, Block G, East Sun Industrial Centre, 16 Shing Yip St, Kwun Tong, Hong Kong

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 3.7 based on 6 reviews

**Website:** https://www.urbanyogahk.com/

**Phone:** +852 6429 9540

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio presents a significant concern regarding hygiene, as reviews highlight dirty equipment and unpleasant odors. The cleanliness of the mats and the studio environment is described as subpar, leading to negative experiences for practitioners who have reported skin sensitivities after use. The need for improvement in maintenance and hygiene protocols is emphasized by multiple reviewers. Recommendations include replacing old mats and ensuring regular cleaning by staff rather than relying on students.

Overall, the atmosphere is marred by these cleanliness issues, which overshadow the potential benefits of a yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

None

## Areas for Improvement

- Dirty environment and equipment.
- Mats are smelly and in poor condition.
- Lack of effective cleaning protocols.
- Negative impact on skin sensitivity for users.
