# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comprehensive 200-hour Teacher Training Course, featuring experienced instructors known for their dedication and expertise in various yoga styles such as Ashtanga and Hatha. The curriculum is well-structured, focusing on creating a supportive learning environment that accommodates students of all skill levels. The studio's staff is praised for their friendliness and helpfulness, contributing to a positive atmosphere. However, concerns regarding facility safety and maintenance have been highlighted, including unsafe props and cleanliness issues. The quality of the food is often noted positively, providing a satvic diet. Overall, it is considered a good option for beginners seeking an authentic yoga experience but may require improvements in facilities and organization.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and knowledgeable instructors.
- Comprehensive and well-structured curriculum.
- Supportive and friendly staff.
- Delicious and healthy satvic food.
- Strong community and opportunities for connection.

## Areas for Improvement

- Facilities have safety issues, including broken props.
- Class sizes often exceed advertised limits, leading to overcrowding.
- Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance of accommodations.
- Disruption to class flow due to lack of adequate props.
- Misrepresentation of facilities on their website.
