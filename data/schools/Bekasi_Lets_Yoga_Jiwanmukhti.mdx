# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and inviting atmosphere reminiscent of Ubud, providing a perfect space for relaxation and rejuvenation. With a diverse range of classes led by skilled instructors, including the highly praised Coach Shinta, participants can find enjoyment in various yoga styles, TRX, belly dance, and pilates. The pricing structure is affordable, with options for single visits or packages, making it accessible for regular attendees. Facilities include a swimming pool, shower rooms, and a gym, although parking accessibility could be improved. Overall, this studio is regarded as one of the most comfortable places to practice yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Relaxing and tranquil atmosphere similar to Ubud
- Affordable pricing and good package deals
- Diverse range of classes including yoga, TRX, and pilates
- Highly praised instructors, particularly Coach Shinta
- Comfortable facilities with swimming pool and gym

## Areas for Improvement

- Parking lot accessibility may be confusing
- Small front parking area
- No clear signage for the spacious back parking lot
