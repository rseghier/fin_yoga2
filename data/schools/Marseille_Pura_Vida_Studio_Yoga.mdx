# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a nurturing and inclusive environment for both students and future instructors. The training program is characterized by a balance of support and high standards, led by knowledgeable teachers, some of whom are trained in physical therapy and osteopathy. The teaching approach emphasizes respect for individual bodies, a non-dogmatic perspective, and fosters critical thinking alongside practice. The variety of classes available at flexible times caters to diverse preferences, making the studio a welcoming space for all practitioners. Overall, the studio is praised for its pleasant atmosphere and enriching experiences in yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Nurturing and inclusive atmosphere
- Experienced teachers, including physical therapists and osteopaths
- Non-dogmatic and respectful approach to yoga
- Variety of classes with flexible scheduling
- Engaging training program for future instructors
