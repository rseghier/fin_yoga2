# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio has been criticized for exhibiting cult-like behaviors, leading to concerns about the influence it has on its members. Reviewers have pointed out tendencies toward exclusivity and a unique language that can alienate individuals from external perspectives. There are alarming claims that the environment promotes infidelity and discourages critical feedback. Overall, the studio appears to be more focused on fulfilling personal desires rather than offering a supportive or healthy yoga experience.
