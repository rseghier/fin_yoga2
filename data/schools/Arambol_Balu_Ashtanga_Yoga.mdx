# Studio Presentation

Located in the tranquil environment of Arambol, this yoga studio offers a unique Ashtanga yoga experience with a focus on personalized practice. The daily Mysore-style sessions are complemented by led classes and workshops, fostering a community that values both individual growth and mutual support. Classes are led by a highly regarded teacher known for his extensive knowledge, energy, and commitment to each student's journey. Reviewers frequently highlight the teacher's ability to connect with students, tailor practices to individual needs, and create a nurturing atmosphere. However, some reviews also mention concerns regarding the attention given to different students during sessions, suggesting a need for a more balanced approach. The studio is praised for its clean and welcoming space, providing a retreat for serious practitioners seeking to deepen their Ashtanga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and dedicated teacher
- Personalized attention tailored to student needs
- Supportive and nurturing atmosphere
- Comprehensive workshops alongside regular classes
- Clean and welcoming environment

## Areas for Improvement

- Uneven attention given to some students over others
- Concerns regarding ethics in teacher-student interactions
