# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a variety of classes in a welcoming atmosphere, making it a great place for relaxation and rejuvenation. With a team of skilled instructors specializing in both yoga and Pilates, participants appreciate the attentive and supportive nature of the teachers. Many reviews highlight the engaging classes, particularly those led by Diane and Dominique, which are known for their unique teaching styles and ability to motivate students. Unfortunately, some reviews mention issues with class cancellations and lack of communication from the staff, leading to frustration among attendees. Overall, the studio is praised for its clean and inviting environment, proximity to nature, and the sense of community it fosters among attendees.

Other strengths include fair pricing options and a range of class schedules to accommodate different preferences.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and inviting atmosphere
- Skilled instructors providing supportive and engaging classes
- Good variety of yoga and Pilates options available
- Clean and well-designed space
- Fair pricing with Urban Sports option

## Areas for Improvement

- Frequent class cancellations and uncommunicated no-shows from teachers
- Occasional lack of staff availability for inquiries
- Unreliable schedule causing inconvenience for students
