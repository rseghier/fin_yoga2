# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene escape in the bustling city, focusing on the classical yoga tradition and promoting holistic wellness for body, mind, and soul. It features a variety of programs, including open classes, structured courses, meditation evenings, and workshops, catering to those seeking a deeper connection to yoga. A special highlight is the opportunity to practice outdoors in the garden during the summer, adding to the peaceful ambience. With a commitment to Sivananda's teachings, this studio provides a nurturing environment for individuals looking to recharge and explore their yoga practice further.

It is an ideal place for both beginners and those looking to deepen their yogic journey.
