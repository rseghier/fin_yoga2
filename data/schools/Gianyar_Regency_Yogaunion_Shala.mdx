# Studio Presentation

Located in the breathtaking rice fields of Ubud, this yoga studio offers a transformative experience for all practitioners, from beginners to aspiring teachers. The knowledgeable and passionate instructors provide exceptional guidance, making every class enriching. With a strong focus on alignment and adjustments, students are empowered to deepen their practice. The supportive community environment fosters connections among participants, creating a sense of belonging. The studio offers high-quality teacher training programs, complemented by delicious vegan meals and comfortable accommodations. However, challenges related to pest management and occasional dissatisfaction with management responses have been noted. Overall, this studio remains a popular choice for those seeking a holistic yoga experience amidst nature.



## Why You'll Love It

- Exceptional and knowledgeable instructors.
- Strong student support and community building.
- Beautiful location surrounded by nature.
- Delicious vegan meals provided.
- Well-structured and comprehensive training programs.

## Areas for Improvement

- Pest management issues leading to health concerns.
- Occasional poor responses from management regarding complaints.
- Inconsistent meal quality reported by some students.
