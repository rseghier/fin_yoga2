# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is located in a peaceful and beautiful setting in Ubud, offering a unique and authentic experience away from typical tourist spots. The instructor is noted for being kind, knowledgeable, and passionate, providing traditional yoga classes that cater to different levels of practitioners. The premises are clean and well-maintained, featuring private rooms with air conditioning. Classes operate on a donation basis for locals, making it accessible, though some foreign visitors found the pricing steep. The studio encourages a community atmosphere, allowing guests to interact with locals. However, reviewers noted some inconsistencies in the class structure and hygiene of equipment, suggesting room for improvement in organization and cleanliness. Overall, it provides a special opportunity for those seeking immersion in local culture through yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Peaceful and beautiful setting in Ubud
- Kind and knowledgeable instructor
- Authentic experience away from tourist spots
- Clean and well-maintained premises
- Affordable for locals with a donation-based model

## Areas for Improvement

- Pricing can be steep for foreign visitors
- Class organization needs improvement
- Yoga mats were noted as unclean and smelly
- Instructor's communication not always clear
- Some students felt the experience did not meet expectations
