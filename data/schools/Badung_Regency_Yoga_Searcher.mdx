# Studio Presentation

The yoga studio offers a unique blend of wellness and relaxation in a serene environment. Reviews highlight its beautiful setting and the quality of the yoga classes, particularly praising talented instructors. The studio's commitment to healthy living is emphasized by the presence of a vegan-friendly restaurant and a well-equipped spa offering a variety of treatments. Although the atmosphere is generally described as peaceful, there are concerns about the upkeep of the facilities, with some reviewers noting issues with cleanliness and maintenance. The location adds to its appeal, being situated close to popular attractions in Uluwatu. However, some inconsistencies in service and value compared to pricing have raised questions among visitors. Overall, the studio is seen as a nice space for yoga and relaxation, but it may require improvements to match the expectations of a premium experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful and tranquil environment
- Talented yoga instructors, particularly Andiasa and Suparta
- Excellent breakfast offerings with vegan options
- Spa services with a variety of treatments
- Convenient location near local attractions

## Areas for Improvement

- Maintenance issues with facilities, including pool and bathrooms
- Inconsistent service quality among staff
- No complimentary yoga classes for hotel guests
- Noise from nearby streets and establishments affecting the experience
- Concerns over cleanliness in some areas
