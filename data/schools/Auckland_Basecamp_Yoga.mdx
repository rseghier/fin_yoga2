**Address:** 18 Westmoreland Street West, Grey Lynn, Auckland 1021, New Zealand

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.9 based on 31 reviews

**Website:** http://www.basecampyoga.co.nz/

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio provides a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere, making it particularly suitable for beginners and parents with young children. The classes, led by various skilled instructors, cater to a diverse range of needs, including trauma-informed yoga and restorative techniques. The space itself is described as beautiful and grounding, contributing to a sense of peace and community among participants. While the studio has received praise for its supportive environment and the strong connections fostered between attendees, some logistical issues have been noted regarding parking and communication. Despite these challenges, many reviews highlight the overall positive experience and the quality of instruction offered.

## Why You'll Love It

- Inclusive and supportive environment, especially for beginner yogis and parents.
- Skilled instructors offering a variety of yoga styles, including trauma-informed practices.
- Beautiful studio space that promotes a sense of peace and community.
- Positive atmosphere with a focus on building relationships among participants.

## Areas for Improvement

- Difficulty in finding parking, leading to challenges in attending classes.
- Poor communication regarding late arrivals and unanswered inquiries.
- Some reviews mention unfriendliness and unhelpfulness in customer service.
