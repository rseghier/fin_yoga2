# Studio Presentation

This studio offers Iyengar Yoga classes emphasizing alignment and precision in postures. Each session follows a structured sequence, allowing practitioners to deepen their understanding of their bodies through the use of supportive props. The teaching approach focuses on individual attention, creating a welcoming atmosphere for both newcomers and experienced yogis.
