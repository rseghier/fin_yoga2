# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is a picturesque retreat located in Tulum, featuring a stunning open-air design surrounded by lush plants. It offers a diverse range of classes from traditional yoga to aerial disciplines such as pole and hoop. The atmosphere is described as welcoming and energetic, making it an ideal place for both relaxation and community gatherings. Visitors appreciate the engaging workshops, including sound baths and unique yoga classes that cater to various levels. The presence of refreshing smoothies and a shop next door adds to the overall experience, enhancing the studio's charm. Many guests have expressed their desire to return, highlighting the studio as one of Tulum's best spots for wellness and yoga activities.



## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful open-air setup surrounded by plants
- Variety of classes including yoga, aerial hoop, and pole
- Welcoming and kind owner
- Amazing workshops and special events
- Delicious smoothies available on-site
