# Studio Presentation

This gym combines a vibrant atmosphere with excellent facilities, including both indoor and outdoor training areas. It is well-equipped with various workout essentials, serving a range of classes such as Hot Pilates and HIIT. The gym is praised for its friendly staff and helpful service, creating a welcoming environment for all fitness enthusiasts. Reviewers particularly enjoy the outdoor space, the relaxed vibe, and the availability of a café for post-workout refreshments. While the gym offers a solid training experience, some users have noted issues with equipment maintenance and class availability, suggesting areas for improvement.

## Why You'll Love It

- Well-equipped indoor and outdoor training areas.
- Friendly and helpful staff.
- Great variety of classes including Hot Pilates and HIIT.
- Relaxed atmosphere with a nice café for refreshments.
- Affordable pricing for class drop-ins.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some equipment is noted to be rusty and requires maintenance.
- Issues with class availability and instructor punctuality.
- Concerns about the practicality of weightlifting platforms inside.
- Occasional overcrowding during peak times.
