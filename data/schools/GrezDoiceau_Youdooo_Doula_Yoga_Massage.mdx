# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and nurturing environment, particularly focused on prenatal and postnatal care. The experienced instructors, Claire and Evrard, are known for their attentive and soothing approach, providing personalized massages and yoga sessions that cater to the needs of expectant and new mothers. Clients appreciate the tranquil setting, which includes a beautiful yurt surrounded by nature, enhancing the overall experience. Many reviews highlight the strong sense of community fostered through classes and groups like Mamans Tisseuses, where parents can share experiences in a supportive atmosphere. The studio's services extend beyond yoga, including personalized massages, making it a holistic wellness space for families. Overall, it is a magical place for relaxation, healing, and connection during the transformative period of motherhood.

## Why You'll Love It

- Attentive and soothing instructors, Claire and Evrard.
