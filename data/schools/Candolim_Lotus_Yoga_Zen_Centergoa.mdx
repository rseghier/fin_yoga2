# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a tranquil rooftop setting perfect for morning yoga sessions, surrounded by palm trees and nature. The classes are led by an exceptionally skilled instructor known for providing personalized guidance and valuable insights, making them suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced yogis. Participants have praised the supportive atmosphere, kind teaching style, and the unique combination of asanas and breathing exercises. Many attendees express gratitude for the positive impact of the sessions, finding both physical and mental rejuvenation. The space is clean and welcoming, with good-quality mats provided, enhancing the overall experience. Overall, it is a wonderful place to practice yoga while enjoying the serene environment of Candolim.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and skilled instructor
- Suitable for all levels of yoga practitioners
- Beautiful rooftop setting surrounded by nature
- Personalized attention and adjustments provided
- Positive and supportive atmosphere

## Areas for Improvement

- Occasionally fast-paced classes
- Some reviews mentioned a lack of flow in instruction
- Challenging to find initially
