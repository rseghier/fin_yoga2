# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is known for its strong sense of community and diverse class offerings, making it suitable for everyone. It serves as a safe space, beautifully designed and emphasizing cleanliness and hygiene. The yoga teacher training programs focus not just on physical practice but also on philosophy and personal growth, enhancing the overall experience for both students and instructors. The welcoming culture and highly skilled instructors contribute positively to the atmosphere, ensuring every visitor feels embraced and acknowledged. The studio provides an extensive variety of classes, including both in-person and online options. Overall, it stands out as a remarkable oasis in the city, dedicated to well-being and personal development.



## Why You'll Love It

- Strong sense of community and belonging
- Diverse class offerings for all levels
- High-quality instructor training programs
- Emphasis on cleanliness and hygiene
- Warm and welcoming atmosphere

## Areas for Improvement

- Additional charge for mat rentals not communicated clearly
- Reception staff appeared disinterested and unhelpful
- Some students felt unwelcome as Urbansports members
