# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a variety of classes led by skilled instructors, ensuring a solid experience for participants. However, there are significant issues regarding subscription management and customer service as evidenced by some negative feedback. Clients have expressed dissatisfaction with billing practices, indicating a lack of communication and resolution when issues arise. Overall, while the instruction may be commendable, potential clients should be wary of potential administrative challenges.
