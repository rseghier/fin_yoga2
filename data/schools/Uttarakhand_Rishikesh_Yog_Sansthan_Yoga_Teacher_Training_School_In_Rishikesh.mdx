# Studio Presentation

This yoga teacher training school offers an enriching experience with highly esteemed instructors, notably Dr. Amaravathi Eraballi, who is praised for her passion and exceptional English skills. Students have expressed gratitude for the foundational knowledge they gained, kickstarting their yoga careers and emphasizing the insightful teaching approach tailored to both beginners and those looking to deepen their practice. While the educational component is highly regarded, some students noted significant disturbances due to noise at night from nearby dogs, impacting their overall experience. Regardless, the time spent at the studio is viewed as invaluable, making it a popular choice for aspiring yoga teachers looking for quality training in a renowned location.



## Why You'll Love It

- Highly esteemed instructors, particularly Dr. Amaravathi Eraballi
- Effective teaching approach suitable for both beginners and advanced practitioners
- Valuable foundational knowledge for launching yoga careers
- Positive student testimonials and recommendations

## Areas for Improvement

- Significant noise disturbances from nearby dogs at night
- Difficulty sleeping due to incessant barking
