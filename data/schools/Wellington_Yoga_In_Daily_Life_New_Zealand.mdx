# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere, perfectly conducive for yoga practice. It operates as an ashram, providing a unique space that is both spacious and cozy, with comfortable carpeted flooring. The studio focuses on a traditional hatha style of yoga combined with meditation methods, allowing practitioners to engage in a holistic exploration of their well-being. Classes are structured to last an hour and a half, encouraging participants to start their day positively and energized. With a commitment to reasonable pricing and social responsibility, a portion of class fees supports charitable organizations in India. The dedicated and knowledgeable teachers create a supportive environment for students of all levels, making this studio a cherished place for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Combination of traditional hatha yoga and meditation
- Spacious and cozy environment
