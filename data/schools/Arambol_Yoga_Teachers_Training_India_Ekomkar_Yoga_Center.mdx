# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a transformative and enriching experience for aspiring teachers through its comprehensive 200-hour Teacher Training Course (TTC). With a strong emphasis on personalized instruction due to small class sizes, the studio fosters a supportive community where students feel at home. The curriculum covers a diversity of yoga styles including Hatha, Ashtanga, and Vinyasa, along with philosophy, anatomy, and meditation practices. Attendees highlight the exceptional quality of the teaching staff, who are approachable and deeply knowledgeable. The tranquil environment near the sea, along with delicious vegetarian meals tailored to the needs of the students, enhances the overall experience. Students not only gain theoretical knowledge but also undergo personal growth and emotional healing during their time at the studio. Many express gratitude for the care and support received, making it a highly recommended destination for yoga teacher training.

## Why You'll Love It

- Personalized instruction due to small class sizes
- Diverse and comprehensive curriculum covering various yoga styles
- Exceptional and caring teaching staff
- Supportive community atmosphere
- Delicious and nutritious vegetarian meals
