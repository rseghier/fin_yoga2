# Studio Presentation

This yoga and pilates studio provides a friendly and welcoming atmosphere, making it a lovely local option for practitioners. With a focus on personalized attention, instructors like Kyami are noted for their patience and knowledge, ensuring a positive experience in classes such as mat pilates. However, the studio's organizational aspects could use improvement, as some reviews mention difficulties navigating the website and a lack of clear information regarding class details and what to bring. Additionally, the arrival experience may be slightly uncomfortable due to crowding at the entrance. Overall, while the studio has a warm community feel, some basic logistical elements require attention to enhance the overall experience.



## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and warm instructors
- Welcoming community atmosphere
- Knowledgeable teaching, especially in mat pilates

## Areas for Improvement

- Confusing website and information about registration
- Lack of clarity about class time and what to bring
- Crowded entrance can create discomfort
- Difficulties in communication via phone
