# Studio Presentation

The studio offers a clean, ambient environment with a beautiful design that contributes to a relaxing and enjoyable experience. Many reviews highlight the professionalism and attentiveness of the instructors, who focus on alignment and provide hands-on adjustments. The studio has a variety of classes including aerials and pilates, with innovative sequences that reflect the unique expertise of the teachers. However, experiences can vary significantly: while some find an encouraging atmosphere, others have reported rudeness from both instructors and fellow attendees. The studio is newly opened, featuring great equipment and a cozy setting that appeals to many. Overall, it appears to be an emerging choice in the area for those looking for a pleasant yoga and pilates experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Clean and ambient environment
- Professional and attentive instructors
- Innovative and varied class offerings
- Great equipment and cozy setting
- Welcoming atmosphere for many attendees

## Areas for Improvement

- Reported rudeness from instructors and members
- Unwelcoming experience for some attendees
- Concerns about inclusivity and safety
- Class conduct can vary significantly
