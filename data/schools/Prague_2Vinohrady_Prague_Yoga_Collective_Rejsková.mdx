# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming and friendly atmosphere that enhances the yoga experience for practitioners. With a diverse selection of quality teachers, the studio helps deepen the practice and foster a love for yoga. Many students appreciate the professionalism and skill of the instructors, with several favorites mentioned. The space is noted for being cozy, accommodating even full classes comfortably. However, some critiques highlight the need for better ventilation in the gym rooms and issues regarding late arrival policies. Overall, it is viewed positively as a favorite spot for yoga enthusiasts in Prague.



## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and friendly atmosphere
- High-quality and diverse teachers
- Cozy space that accommodates full classes well
- Positive experiences leading to deepened practice

## Areas for Improvement

- Tiny changing rooms
- Rental fee for yoga mats (50 Kč)
- Lack of fresh air in gym rooms
- Issues with late arrival policies
