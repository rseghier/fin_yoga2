# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and authentic environment for Ashtanga practitioners, led by experienced teachers Iain and Allen. The space, while not vast, exudes positive energy and a sense of community. Many practitioners feel a strong sense of peace and support during their practice. The studio is known for its commitment to traditional Ashtanga Mysore style, making it a valuable destination for both beginners and advanced yogis alike. There is a strong camaraderie among students, contributing to the nurturing atmosphere. Classes are designed to accommodate individualized learning, with teachers providing excellent attention and guidance. Registration is flexible, allowing for day-to-day participation. Overall, the studio is revered for its warm environment and teaching quality, making it a special place for yoga in Bali.



## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and attentive teachers, Iain and Allen.
- Welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.
- Strong sense of community among practitioners.
- Flexible registration options for classes.
- Focus on traditional Ashtanga Mysore style.

## Areas for Improvement

- Not suitable for absolute beginners looking to learn.
- Some reviews indicate a lack of warmth in initial experiences.
- Space may feel limited to some practitioners.
