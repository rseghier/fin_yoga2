# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming and tranquil environment that caters to both locals and travelers. Classes are conducted in both Japanese and English, accommodating a diverse range of participants. Instructors are known for their thoughtful approach, emphasizing personal attention and offering various styles, including Vinyasa and Hatha. The studio is equipped with a variety of props, enhancing the experience for beginners and more seasoned practitioners alike. After sessions, guests can enjoy a selection of herbal teas in a cozy shop below. Overall, it's a holistic space that encourages connection with breath and body, making it a perfect spot for yoga enthusiasts.



## Why You'll Love It

- Bilingual instruction available in English and Japanese
- Friendly and welcoming instructors and staff
- Variety of yoga styles offered, including Vinyasa and Hatha
- Availability of yoga props and equipment
- Cozy atmosphere with a cafe and shop on-site

## Areas for Improvement

- Some classes can be fully booked, requiring reservations
- Limited availability for those who do not speak Japanese in some classes
- One review noted an uncomfortable experience in the shop
