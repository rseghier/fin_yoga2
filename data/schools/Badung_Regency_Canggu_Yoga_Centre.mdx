# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio located in Pererenan, Badung Regency, Bali, offers a beautiful and modern space for yoga practice, with a focus on both heated and non-heated classes. The studio is clean, spacious, and surrounded by lush greenery, creating a calming atmosphere. Staff and instructors are highlighted as friendly and experienced, providing a welcoming experience for participants of all levels. With a diverse range of classes, including Bikram yoga, hot Pilates, and vinyasa, there is something for everyone. The on-site cafe serves healthy, delicious food and drinks, making it a one-stop destination for wellness. However, some reviews express dissatisfaction regarding customer service and the authenticity of certain yoga offerings, particularly concerning Bikram yoga. Overall, it's a great place to practice and unwind in a serene environment.

## Why You'll Love It

- Clean and spacious facilities with beautiful views
- Friendly and experienced instructors
- Diverse range of yoga classes, including heated options
- Welcoming atmosphere and helpful staff
- On-site cafe with healthy food and drinks

## Areas for Improvement

- Some customers reported poor customer service
- Issues with authenticity in the Bikram yoga offerings
- Reported unpleasant odors from the studio flooring
- Occasional noise from nearby construction
- Exclusive or snobbish attitudes from some staff members
