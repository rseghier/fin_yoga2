Heartland Yoga is a transformative yoga studio located in Goa, India, offering Kundalini and Tantra yoga teacher training.

**Address:** House No 28, near Ajoba Temple, Talavada, Querim, Goa 403524, India

**Category:** Yoga retreat center

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a diverse range of teacher training programs and classes, primarily focusing on Kundalini and Tantra yoga. The instructors are noted for their deep knowledge and supportive guidance, creating a nurturing environment for both personal growth and professional development. Students have praised the studio's beautiful surroundings, which include views of the jungle and nearby beach, contributing to a serene and inspirational atmosphere. Accommodation is often highlighted as comfortable, and meals are provided with an emphasis on healthy and sattvic options. However, there are significant concerns raised regarding management practices, especially related to class organization and professionalism. While some experiences were transformative and empowering, others noted unethical behavior from the administration, creating a mixed reputation.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and knowledgeable instructors
- Beautiful location near the beach and jungle
- Focus on personal growth and community atmosphere
- High-quality, healthy meals provided
- Transformative experiences reported by many students

## Areas for Improvement

- Disorganization in class scheduling
- Unethical management practices reported
- Issues with accommodation hygiene in some cases
- Excessive demands for additional payments from students
- Threats and intimidation from administration mentioned
