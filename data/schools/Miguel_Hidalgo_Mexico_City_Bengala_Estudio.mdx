# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is located in a vibrant area of Mexico City. However, it has received criticism for its lack of hospitality and amenities. The temperature in the studio is described as too dry, lacking the necessary humidity for comfort. Additionally, the men's showers are shared, which compromises privacy. The absence of basic offerings such as fresh water and soap further detracts from the overall experience. Overall, the feedback suggests that improvements in customer service and facilities could enhance the overall satisfaction of attendees.
