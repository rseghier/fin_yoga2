# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a space where attendees can enjoy a rigorous hot yoga experience and focus on their physical fitness. The facility is praised for its cleanliness, creating a pleasant atmosphere for practice. However, there are some concerns regarding maintenance and equipment, as some machines are reported to be old and not in working condition. Reviewers have expressed a desire for improved amenities in the changing rooms and better overall staff responsiveness. Despite some positive feedback about the yoga experience, the lack of cleanliness in certain areas and the inattentive staff have been significant deterrents. The recent renovations have not addressed these issues adequately, leading to dissatisfaction and a decision to leave the studio by some members.

## Why You'll Love It

- Clean and pleasant atmosphere
- Effective hot yoga classes that help with weight loss
- Strong engagement from attendees who appreciate the practices

## Areas for Improvement

- Old and poorly maintained equipment
- Inadequate cleanliness in changing rooms
- Unresponsive and inattentive staff
- Narrow gym area with poor layout for social distancing
- Lack of proper amenities like modern dryers and shower facilities
