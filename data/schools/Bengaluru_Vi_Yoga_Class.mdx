# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Bengaluru is known for its welcoming atmosphere and experienced instructors. Many reviewers appreciate the diverse range of classes offered, catering to all levels of practitioners from beginners to advanced. The studio's cleanliness and well-maintained facilities contribute to a positive experience. Additionally, students highlight the community feel, where they feel supported and encouraged in their practice.

However, some visitors have noted that class sizes can be larger than preferred, making it challenging to receive individual attention. Despite this, the overall vibe and the quality of instruction keep students coming back for more.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming atmosphere
- Experienced instructors
- Diverse range of classes
- Clean and well-maintained facilities
- Strong community feel

## Areas for Improvement

- Larger class sizes can limit individual attention
- Limited parking options
