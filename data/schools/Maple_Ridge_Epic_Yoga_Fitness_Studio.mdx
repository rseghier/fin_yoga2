# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Maple Ridge offers a warm and friendly atmosphere, perfect for practitioners of all levels. The instructors are highly knowledgeable, providing personalized attention and guidance across various yoga styles, including yin and Hatha. Many reviews highlight the community feel of the studio, promoting wellness and connection among members. Classes are well-structured and aim to cater to individual needs, ensuring both relaxation and invigoration during sessions. Additionally, there are various fitness classes, including Pilates, enhancing the fitness options available to attendees. Some feedback suggests that temperature control during certain classes could be improved for overall comfort. Overall, this studio is a welcoming haven for those looking to deepen their practice and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm, community-centered atmosphere
- Knowledgeable and attentive instructors
- Challenging yet accessible classes for all levels
- Personalized attention and clear guidance
- Great variety of classes including yoga and Pilates

## Areas for Improvement

- Temperature can be too cold during certain classes
- Some found the name 'Epic' misleading regarding class intensity
