# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio emphasizes the transformative power of traditional Hatha Yoga practices, particularly Surya Kriya and Angamardana, taught by the experienced teacher Bimbisara. Students express deep gratitude for the supportive and patient teaching style, fostering a calm and welcoming environment.
