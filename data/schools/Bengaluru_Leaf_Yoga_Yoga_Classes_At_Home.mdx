# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio claims to offer home yoga classes, but reviews indicate a troubling pattern of fraudulent behavior regarding payments. Some customers have reported difficulties in communication after making advance payments, leading them to feel scammed. However, there is a contrasting experience from at least one individual who praised their personal yoga classes with a professional teacher, noting satisfaction with the training received. Overall, the studio appears to have serious trust issues among clients, which is critical for any service-oriented business.

In summary, potential customers should exercise caution before proceeding with payments due to reports of unprofessional conduct and lack of responsiveness from the studio.
