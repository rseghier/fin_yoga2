# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, ideal for practitioners of all levels. It features a variety of classes including Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Kundalini, and prenatal yoga, ensuring there's something for everyone. The instructors, led by the attentive and knowledgeable Julia, who is also an osteopath, create a supportive environment where students can thrive. With three daily class options and regular workshops, the studio fosters a sense of community and personal growth. Many reviews note the beautiful and serene space, enhancing the practice experience. Overall, this studio is viewed as a top choice for yoga enthusiasts in the city, known for its quality instruction and positive energy.



## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and attentive instructors, particularly Julia, who is both a yoga teacher and an osteopath.
- A variety of yoga styles available, including Vinyasa, Ashtanga, and Kundalini.
- A warm and inviting atmosphere that fosters community and personal growth.
- Regular workshops and retreats that enhance the overall yoga experience.
- Beautiful, serene studio environment that helps practitioners feel at peace.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some reviews mentioned discomfort due to incense burning during classes.
- A suggestion that the studio could benefit from more diverse offerings or instructors.
- Limited information on class size and capacity.
