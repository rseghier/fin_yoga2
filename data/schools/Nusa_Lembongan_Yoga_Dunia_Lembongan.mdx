# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a beautiful and peaceful environment ideal for both beginners and advanced practitioners. With a variety of classes including Vinyasa, Yin, and Aerial Yoga, the instruction is personalized due to intimate class sizes. The knowledgeable instructors create a welcoming atmosphere where students feel supported in their practice. Facilities include a lovely studio with an inviting open space and a pool, enhancing the overall experience. Additionally, students rave about the delicious breakfast offered during teacher training programs, making the experience even more enjoyable. Its location on Nusa Lembongan adds to its charm, providing a serene setting for a fulfilling yoga journey.

The community at the studio is described as warm and friendly, making it a perfect getaway for yoga enthusiasts looking to immerse themselves in their practice while enjoying the beautiful island.

## Why You'll Love It

- Peaceful and beautiful environment
- Personalized attention due to small class sizes
- Knowledgeable and supportive instructors
- Delicious breakfast included during training
- Diverse class offerings including Vinyasa, Yin, and Aerial Yoga

## Areas for Improvement

- Some classes can be very hot due to open-air setup
- Distractions from music during classes
- Limited ventilation with only one small fan
