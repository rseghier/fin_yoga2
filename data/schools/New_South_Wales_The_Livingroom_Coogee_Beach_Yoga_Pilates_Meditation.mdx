# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a tranquil and inviting atmosphere, enhanced by its proximity to Coogee Beach. The studio is praised for its comfortable setup, including air conditioning and ample space for classes. The owner is noted for her strong support of the community and the wellness initiatives she provides, such as complementary yoga classes. Users appreciate the variety of teachers and classes available, contributing to a positive overall experience. However, potential new customers may face challenges with the booking system, as there are no clear instructions or immediate assistance available. Some reviews highlight a lack of communication resources, which can lead to frustration when classes are full.

Overall, the studio fosters a positive environment for yoga and well-being, making it a preferred choice for many.

## Why You'll Love It

- Amazing energy and peaceful vibe near the beach
- Supportive owner who promotes community wellness
- Comfortable space with good facilities
- Variety of classes and teachers available
- Positive feedback from participants in special sessions

## Areas for Improvement

- Lack of clear instructions on joining classes
- Difficulties with booking system and app assistance
- No immediate contact number for inquiries
- Turning away clients when classes are full
