# Studio Presentation

This studio offers excellent Ashtanga Yoga classes, including both Mysore style and guided sessions. The instructor is praised for her extensive experience and dedication, providing personalized attention, especially to beginners. The environment is described as beautiful and spacious, with ample natural light, creating a welcoming atmosphere for practitioners. Classes are tailored to accommodate all levels, encouraging students to deepen their practice. The studio is highly recommended for its supportive and warm community, making it an ideal place for anyone looking to start or enhance their yoga journey.

Flor, the instructor, is noted for her passion and care, ensuring that every student feels valued and guided throughout their practice.
