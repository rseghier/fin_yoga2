# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming atmosphere with beautiful and well-designed spaces filled with natural light. It features a fantastic rooftop area for classes during the summer, providing stunning views of the Berlin skyline. Many reviews commend the knowledgeable and friendly instructors who create an inclusive environment for students of all levels. The studio has a wide variety of classes, including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Calisthenics, ensuring that there is something for everyone. While most experiences are positive, some reviewers mention issues with overcrowding and cleanliness in the studios, which can detract from the overall experience. Despite these drawbacks, the strong community vibe and the availability of tea and refreshments make it a beloved spot for many practitioners.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and friendly atmosphere
- Knowledgeable and supportive instructors
- Stunning rooftop classes with great views
- Variety of classes for all levels and preferences
- Clean and beautifully designed spaces

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes can be overcrowded
- Cleanliness issues reported in some reviews
- Limited space for certain movements during peak times
- Confusion regarding class bookings and facility use
- Occasional mismatch between booked and received classes
