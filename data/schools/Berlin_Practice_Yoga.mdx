# Studio Presentation

This cozy yoga studio in Berlin offers a welcoming community atmosphere, fostering a sense of belonging among its practitioners. The studio is praised for its inspirational instructors who lead engaging classes that cater to various skill levels, incorporating everything from basic asanas to more advanced practices, including unique offerings like mantra singing and acupuncture during Savasana. Weekend workshops are also available, making it a hub for deeper exploration of yoga. However, some reviews mention that the studio can get quite warm, impacting comfort levels during sessions. Overall, it is regarded as a beloved place for many who appreciate both the teachings and the inviting environment.



## Why You'll Love It

- Cozy and inviting community atmosphere
- Inspirational and encouraging instructors
- Variety of classes including basic asanas and Open Classes
- Unique offerings like mantra singing and acupuncture
- Regular weekend workshops

## Areas for Improvement

- Studio can get too warm during classes
- Some reviews mention insufficient air circulation
