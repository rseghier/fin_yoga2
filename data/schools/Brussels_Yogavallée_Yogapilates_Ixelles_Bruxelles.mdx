# Studio Presentation

This yoga and Pilates studio in Brussels offers a welcoming atmosphere with a variety of quality classes led by attentive and caring instructors. The spacious and well-ventilated rooms contribute to a pleasant practice environment. Many students appreciate the diverse offerings and the personal attention received during sessions. However, some reviews note the limited changing facilities, which can make transitions between classes challenging. Overall, the studio is seen as a great place for regular practice, fostering a sense of community among participants.



## Why You'll Love It

- Qualified and attentive instructors
- Welcoming and caring atmosphere
- Variety of classes available
- Spacious and clean facilities

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited changing facilities
- Crowded spaces during classes
- Classes primarily taught in English may be challenging for some
