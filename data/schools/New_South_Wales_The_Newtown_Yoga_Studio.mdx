# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and inviting atmosphere, situated just off King street. Known for its small class sizes, it provides personalized attention, making it ideal for beginners. The studio specializes in the Iyengar method, focusing on the alignment of poses and body movement, which helps students experience significant insight into their practice. With instructors who are attentive to individual needs, the studio is praised for creating a supportive environment where students can thrive and develop their yoga skills. Many long-term attendees appreciate the consistent and understanding guidance, making it a favored location for a gentle introduction to yoga.
