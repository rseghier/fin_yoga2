# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers an exceptional Ashtanga yoga experience set in the serene backdrop of Agonda Beach, Goa. With a highly qualified teacher who is both kind and professional, students of all levels are welcomed and encouraged to deepen their practice. The classes, including Mysore style, are complemented by the beautiful natural surroundings and a tranquil atmosphere that enhances the overall experience. Participants can enjoy a delicious vegetarian breakfast included with their classes, fostering a sense of community among students. The positive energy and dedicated instruction provided create a unique environment where students can connect with their practice and themselves.

Overall, this studio combines expert teaching, a supportive environment, and beautiful scenery, making it a must-visit destination for anyone seeking personal growth through yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly experienced and kind teacher who connects well with students
- Beautiful and peaceful setting surrounded by nature and views of the sea
- Complimentary delicious vegetarian breakfast included with classes
- Inclusive environment for all skill levels, providing personalized support
- Strong sense of community among practitioners

## Areas for Improvement

- Breakfast could benefit from more variety daily
- Some students feel that staying longer would have enhanced their experience
