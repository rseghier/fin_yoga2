# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a highly personalized and supportive environment, with a focus on a variety of yoga styles led by a dedicated team of experienced instructors, notably Geeta Ma'am. Students have praised the interactive and comprehensive nature of the classes, which include not only physical yoga practices but also meditation, chanting, and even Bhagavad Gita teachings. The studio is known for its flexibility in scheduling, allowing attendees to join different batches and utilize online classes. The atmosphere is described as peaceful and energetic, with a strong emphasis on individual attention and community spirit. Many students report significant improvements in their physical health and mental well-being, making it a well-recommended choice for yoga enthusiasts.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and knowledgeable instructors, primarily Geeta Ma'am.
- Flexible scheduling options with opportunities for both in-person and online classes.
- Comprehensive approach to yoga, incorporating meditation, chanting, and personal health guidance.
- Positive and energizing atmosphere that promotes community and personal growth.
- Highly rated by students for improvements in health and well-being.

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited availability for sessions with Geeta Ma'am, typically only on weekends.
- Higher costs for weekend sessions compared to weekdays.
- Some concerns about billing practices and communication regarding payments.
- Student complaints about perceived manipulation in enrolled courses.
- Inconsistency in class availability due to health issues of instructors.
