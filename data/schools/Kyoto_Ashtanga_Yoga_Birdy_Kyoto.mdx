# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming and supportive environment, perfect for both locals and visitors. With a focus on traditional Ashtanga yoga, the classes are led by attentive and caring instructors who provide helpful adjustments and guidance. Many reviewers have noted the sense of community and warmth among the students and teachers alike. The studio operates on a cash-only basis and does not require reservations, making it accessible for drop-in students. Overall, it has received high praise for the quality of instruction and the transformative experience it provides to practitioners.



## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and supportive community atmosphere
- Caring and attentive teachers
- Effective hands-on adjustments and guidance
- Flexible drop-in policy without reservations
- Transformative yoga experience for practitioners

## Areas for Improvement

- Cash-only payment policy
- Limited information on the schedule or class types
