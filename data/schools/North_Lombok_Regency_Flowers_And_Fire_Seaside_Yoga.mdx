# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and beautiful setting for yoga enthusiasts, located by the ocean in North Lombok Regency. With a focus on community and welcoming experiences, the studio features a variety of classes led by skilled teachers like Sumiko, who are praised for their gentle guidance and ability to cater to all skill levels. Participants can enjoy their sessions in a picturesque garden or overlooking the sea, creating a magical atmosphere accentuated by stunning views, including sunset flows. The studio is also known for its healing sessions, such as sound healing, which enhance the overall wellness experience. Many visitors express a desire to return, highlighting the positive energy and unique environment that make this studio a memorable destination for yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Stunning location with beautiful ocean views
- Experienced and kind instructors, especially Sumiko
- Welcoming and community-oriented atmosphere
- Variety of classes including sound healing and sunset flows
- High energy and positive vibes

## Areas for Improvement

- Cash only payments
- Recently opened, limited reviews available
- Need for more class offerings or extended stays
