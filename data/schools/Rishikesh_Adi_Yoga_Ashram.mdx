# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is located in a serene area of Rishikesh, surrounded by the breathtaking Himalayas, providing a tranquil environment for yoga and meditation. Students have praised the warm and inviting atmosphere, characterized by friendly and attentive staff. The facility boasts clean, comfortable accommodations and well-equipped yoga rooms. The school offers a variety of teacher training courses, including 200-hour and 300-hour programs, with highly knowledgeable instructors who are committed to supporting student growth both in practice and teaching. The food is consistently described as delicious and nutritious, catering to vegetarian and vegan diets. Excursions to local attractions and holistic activities enhance the learning experience, making this studio a nurturing sanctuary for personal development and yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and supportive teachers
- Delicious and nutritious vegetarian food
- Clean and comfortable accommodation
- Nurturing and friendly atmosphere
- Excellent facilities and well-maintained yoga rooms

## Areas for Improvement

- Noise from nearby construction during some courses
- Confusion regarding location changes during sessions
- Limited common spaces for relaxation
- Courses may not always meet the expected hour count
