# Studio Presentation

Located in a serene setting, this yoga studio offers a welcoming atmosphere for practitioners of all levels. The accommodating yoga instructor, originally from Australia, has received praise for her kindness and inclusive teaching approach. The studio is conveniently situated next to a well-reviewed vegetarian café, enhancing the overall experience for health-conscious visitors. While the yoga classes are highly regarded, some amenities like Wi-Fi and room conditions do not meet higher expectations. The environment is complemented by the presence of friendly cats and beautiful bungalows nestled in a garden, creating a unique and relaxing experience for guests. Overall, it caters well to those seeking a holistic retreat, though with some areas for improvement.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and inclusive yoga instructor
- Great location next to a vegetarian café
- Positive atmosphere with friendly cats
- Comfortable bungalows blending with nature
- Good value for yoga sessions offered

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited amenities in rooms
- Weak Wi-Fi connectivity
- Overpriced compared to other classes
- Mats had an unpleasant smell
- Pool cleanliness is a concern
