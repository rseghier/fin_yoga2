# Studio Presentation

This yoga and meditation center is situated in a peaceful location on Gili Air, surrounded by lush gardens and a lovely pool area. The facility is well-regarded for its friendly and accommodating staff, who contribute to a warm atmosphere. It offers a variety of yoga classes, including Vinyasa, Hatha, and Aqua Yoga, along with unique moon ceremonies and wellness workshops. The instructors are described as knowledgeable and supportive, making it a great choice for both beginners and experienced yogis. There is also a charming café that serves healthy food and excellent coffee. Despite some occasional criticisms regarding accommodation cleanliness and pricing, many visitors appreciate the overall experience and are drawn back for its tranquil environment and quality classes.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and helpful staff
- Diverse range of yoga classes and wellness workshops
- Beautiful, peaceful location with gardens and pool
- Excellent food options in the café
- Good value for wellness packages

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconsistent cleanliness in accommodation
- Some staff members at reception lack friendliness
- Breakfast options can be bland
- Issues with pests reported by guests
- Occasional dissatisfaction with specific yoga instructors
