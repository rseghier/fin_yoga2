# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a well-maintained space that many reviews appreciate. However, there are significant concerns regarding the instructor's conduct, particularly after the pandemic, which have impacted the overall experience. Some students report a lack of understanding and flexibility concerning class rescheduling and refunds due to health issues. The instructor's attitude has been described as presumptuous, which detracts from the welcoming atmosphere expected in a yoga environment. Overall, while the physical space is praised, the interactions with the instructor have led to disappointing experiences for some clients.

## Why You'll Love It

- Well-maintained physical space
- Positive environment mentioned by some
- Engaging atmosphere in classes

## Areas for Improvement

- Instructor's attitude and behavior criticized
- Issues with class rescheduling and refunds
- Some reports of a lack of empathy from staff
