Roots Helsinki is a yoga studio and café located in Helsinki, Vallila.

**Address:** LH 207B, Eurantie 8-10, 00550 Helsinki, Finland

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.6 based on 111 reviews

**Website:** http://www.rootshki.fi/

**Phone:** +358 40 1438600

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio and café offers a cozy and friendly atmosphere ideal for relaxation and rejuvenation. The studio is designed with a charming and inviting decor, complemented by a beautiful outdoor terrace. While the café serves a variety of delicious options, including vegan treats, it also boasts exceptional coffee. The yoga studio, though on the smaller side, provides a warm environment with a good selection of classes ranging from yin to vinyasa flow. Facilities like changing rooms and the option to borrow mats enhance the experience, but feedback suggests that class sizes can be too large, making it difficult for participants to follow along comfortably.

Overall, it is a place that combines great food and beverages with a unique yoga experience, placing a strong emphasis on community and relaxation.

## Why You'll Love It

- Cozy and inviting atmosphere
- Great selection of vegan options
- Highly praised coffee and baked goods
- Variety of yoga classes offered
- Professional instructors

## Areas for Improvement

- Class sizes can be too large, making movement difficult
- Yoga studio is on the smaller side
