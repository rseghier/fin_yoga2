# Studio Presentation

Located in Hamburg, this yoga studio offers a welcoming environment and a variety of classes that cater to different needs. The strength of the instructors shines through, with many reviews highlighting their patience and support, especially in prenatal classes. The studio emphasizes healthy body alignment across all sessions, ensuring that participants not only enjoy the workouts but also benefit physically and mentally. Classes in English enhance accessibility for a diverse clientele. While the atmosphere is praised, there are concerns regarding timely entry to classes and surprise fees for no-shows, which may impact the overall experience for some members.

Overall, it positions itself as a top choice in the Ottensen area, offering a blend of yoga and workout styles in a pleasant setting.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming atmosphere with friendly instructors
- Variety of classes including prenatal, yoga, Pilates, and Barre
- Focus on healthy body alignment
- Classes available in English
- Highly regarded by local attendees

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with access to classes on time leading to no-show fees
- Some concern about missed entry despite arriving on time
