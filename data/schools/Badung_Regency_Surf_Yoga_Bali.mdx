Surf & Yoga Bali is a welcoming retreat in Canggu that combines yoga, surfing, and community spirit.

**Address:** Gang Melati, Desa Kacamatan No.7, Banjar, Tibubeneng, Kec. Kuta Utara, Kabupaten Badung, Bali 80361, Indonesia

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 4.9 based on 235 reviews

**Website:** http://www.surfnyogacanggu.com/

**Phone:** +62 822-3537-5558

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio and retreat offers a holistic experience blending yoga, surfing, and community vibes. Guests consistently highlight the exceptionally friendly staff, who create a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. The delicious vegetarian meals prepared by the kitchen team are a highlight, with many reviews praising the freshness and taste. The studio features a serene setting away from the busyness of nearby tourist spots, making it a peaceful escape. Daily yoga classes cater to various styles, including Vinyasa and Yin, providing both energizing and calming sessions. While the facilities are noted to be clean and stylish, some guests mention minor issues with room conditions and accessibility, given the studio's location.

## Why You'll Love It

- Incredibly friendly and welcoming staff
- Delicious and fresh vegetarian meals
- Variety of yoga classes and workshops offered
- Clean and stylish facilities
- Strong sense of community and warmth

## Areas for Improvement

- Location is somewhat isolated and not very walkable
- Some rooms reported minor maintenance issues
- Accessibility to the beach can be challenging due to traffic
- Noise from nearby construction mentioned by guests
