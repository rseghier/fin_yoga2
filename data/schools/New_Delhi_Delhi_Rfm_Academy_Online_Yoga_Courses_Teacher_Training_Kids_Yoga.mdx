# Studio Presentation

This academy offers a variety of online yoga courses, including Kids Yoga and teacher training programs. Students appreciate the knowledgeable and supportive instructors, particularly praising the clarity of teaching in both English and Hindi. The courses are designed to be informative and engaging, with a good balance of theory and practical application. Many reviews highlight the transformative experience and personal growth students achieve through their training. However, there have been reports of management issues, such as problems with communication and inconsistencies in service, including difficulties in obtaining class recordings and dissatisfaction with class sizes. Overall, the academy is noted for its well-structured courses and the dedication of its teaching staff.

Courses like the 200 hours YTTC have received positive feedback for their educational value and supportive community atmosphere, although some reviews point to shortcomings in management practices.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and supportive instructors
- Well-structured courses with a balance of theory and practice
- Positive transformative experiences reported by students
- Classes available in both English and Hindi

## Areas for Improvement

- Management issues and poor communication reported
- Inconsistencies in service, such as missing class recordings
- Large class sizes leading to less individual attention
- Complaints about fees and perceived unfair pricing practices
