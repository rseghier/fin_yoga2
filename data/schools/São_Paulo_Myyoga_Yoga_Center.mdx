# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making it an excellent place for practitioners of all levels. With highly qualified instructors, the studio emphasizes not just physical postures, but also incorporates breathing techniques, meditation, and a holistic approach to yoga. The environment is clean and inviting, contributing to an enjoyable experience. Many reviews highlight the studio's careful attention to students and the sense of community fostered here. Additional amenities such as vegan food options and refreshment services for delivery personnel enhance the studio's appeal. Overall, this studio stands out for its supportive environment and a comprehensive yoga experience that goes beyond the mat.

Located in São Paulo, it offers classes like Hatha and Ashtanga Vinyasa, delivering both physical challenges and inspirational practices.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly qualified and attentive instructors
- Welcoming and warm atmosphere
- Offers a comprehensive yoga experience including meditation and pranayama
- Clean and well-maintained facilities
- Excellent vegan food options available

## Areas for Improvement

- Inconvenient shower facilities
- Some reports of poor customer service
