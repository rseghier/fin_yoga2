# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers immersive teacher training experiences that foster personal growth and a deep understanding of yoga. The teachers, known for their warmth and expertise, create a supportive environment for students of all levels. Reviews highlight the studio's beautiful location in Mandrem, Goa, which is a peaceful retreat just a short walk from the beach. The facility features spacious shalas, well-maintained accommodations, and a pool, ensuring a serene atmosphere for practice and relaxation. The curriculum is comprehensive, covering various yoga aspects including asana, anatomy, philosophy, and meditation, tailored for both beginners and advanced practitioners. The emphasis on community and hospitality makes students feel welcomed and engaged throughout their stay. Overall, this yoga school is highly recommended for those seeking personal transformation through yoga education.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and supportive teachers who are experts in their fields.
- Beautiful and tranquil location close to the beach.
- Comprehensive and well-structured curriculum covering all aspects of yoga.
- High standards of cleanliness and comfortable accommodations.
- Delicious and diverse vegetarian food options.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some classes experienced lower than promised contact hours.
- Inconsistencies in food offerings not aligning with Ayurvedic teachings.
- Noise from nearby construction affecting the practice environment.
- Some students felt the asana practice was not as intensive as expected.
- Lack of personalized attention during some advanced courses.
