# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a unique experience with its use of Italian lava stones for heating, providing a warm and comfortable environment. The instructors are known for their friendliness and attentiveness, making it easy for beginners to feel welcomed and guided. The studio maintains a high level of cleanliness, contributing to a pleasant atmosphere for practice. Numerous classes are available throughout the week, including various yoga styles and fitness programs like kickboxing and HIIT, catering to a broad range of interests and fitness levels. Additionally, the convenient location near Tachikawa station makes it accessible for those with busy schedules. Many reviews highlight the refreshing feeling after each session, promoting both physical and mental well-being. The community aspect, with events and gatherings, also enriches the experience for participants.

## Why You'll Love It

- Use of Italian lava stones for a unique heating experience
- Friendly and attentive instructors
- High cleanliness standards
- Wide variety of classes available
- Conveniently located near Tachikawa station

## Areas for Improvement

- Some users found it slightly difficult to enter at night due to lighting
- Inconsistent scheduling issue for specific classes
