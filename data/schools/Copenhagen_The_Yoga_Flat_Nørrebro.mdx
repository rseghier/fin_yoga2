# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is well-regarded for its experienced teaching team and intelligently sequenced classes that incorporate philosophy and mindfulness. It offers a welcoming atmosphere with ample classroom space, free mat hire, and hot tea available during sessions. Classes are conducted in English, catering to both locals and visitors from abroad. The studio is appreciated for its reasonable pricing and community feel, making it a favored choice among practitioners in Copenhagen. Special workshops and private sessions are also well-received, highlighting the personalized approach to instruction. Overall, it is a beautiful space that fosters a supportive yoga community.


## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced teaching team with a focus on mindfulness and philosophy
- Welcoming atmosphere with free mat hire and hot tea
- Classes taught in English, accommodating diverse students
- Reasonably priced for Copenhagen
- Strong community feel with positive feedback from clients

## Areas for Improvement

- Can be hard to find, especially on a first visit
- Strict policy on lateness, with doors locked at class start time
- Some reviews indicate a need for clearer directions or signage
