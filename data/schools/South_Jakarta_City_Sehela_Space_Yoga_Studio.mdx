# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in South Jakarta offers a welcoming and serene atmosphere, perfect for beginners and experienced yogis alike. It features spacious areas flooded with natural light and a variety of amenities, including multiple shower rooms and rental props. The studio excels in cleanliness and provides quality equipment for classes. With a cozy coffee shop located beneath it, members can enjoy refreshments after their practice. Affordable pricing and consistent weekly schedules make it an attractive choice for locals. While the proximity to MRT Fatmawati adds convenience, some areas, like the restrooms, may need maintenance attention to enhance the overall experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Spacious studio with natural lighting
- Variety of classes suitable for all levels
- Clean facilities with quality yoga equipment
- Convenient location near MRT Fatmawati
- Affordable class prices with consistent scheduling

## Areas for Improvement

- Restroom maintenance issues leading to water accumulation
- Limited to one yoga class type
- Distance may be a concern for some attendees
