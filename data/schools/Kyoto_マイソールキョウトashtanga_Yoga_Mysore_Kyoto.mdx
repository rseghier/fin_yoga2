# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a spacious environment located near Nijo Castle, making it a serene place to practice. The classes, particularly the Monday 11am Ashtanga session, are 80 minutes long and focus on half primary series, providing an enriching experience. The instructor, who has been leading the studio for 15 years, is highly attentive and skilled in offering personalized guidance, ensuring that students feel both supported and challenged. The dual-language instruction (English and Japanese) caters to a diverse group, enhancing the overall learning experience. Many students express their satisfaction and a desire to return, indicating a strong sense of community and professionalism.
