# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a beautiful and serene environment in Ubud, surrounded by stunning natural views that enhance the practice experience. It features a friendly and welcoming community, with knowledgeable teachers who inspire students through various yoga styles, including Bikram and Yin yoga. Guests also appreciate the nutritious cafe on-site, which complements their wellness journey with healthy meal options. However, some reviews mention issues such as unresponsive management and cleanliness problems with equipment, which can detract from an otherwise positive experience. Many visitors highlight the exceptional hospitality of the staff, contributing to an overall warm atmosphere, yet challenges in communication may make newcomers feel less welcomed. Despite these concerns, the studio is regarded as a solid choice for yoga enthusiasts seeking a tranquil and uplifting space to practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Stunning natural views and serene environment
- Friendly and knowledgeable teachers who inspire students
- Nutritious food options available on-site
- Welcoming international community of yogis
- Variety of classes offered for different skill levels

## Areas for Improvement

- Management has been unresponsive to customer concerns
- Cleanliness issues with mats and equipment
- Loud environment outside of classes can disrupt meditation
- Some classes are not true to their advertised styles
- Staff communication may feel lacking for newcomers
