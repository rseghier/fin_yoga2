# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a unique experience in Nanaimo. However, recent feedback highlights a concerning lack of empathy towards newcomers. One reviewer reported feeling unwelcome due to being asked to leave a class over a minor coughing incident, which significantly impacted their first yoga experience. Such feedback suggests that the studio may need to improve its approach to accommodating students, especially those who are new to yoga. The atmosphere may be less inclusive than desired, leaving first-time visitors feeling discouraged rather than encouraged in their yoga journey.

Overall, while the studio may have its strengths, the customer service experience can greatly affect potential attendees' willingness to return.
