# Studio Presentation

This donation-based yoga studio in Mexico City offers a welcoming and inclusive environment for practitioners of all levels. With a focus on community, the studio organizes charitable events and provides a variety of classes mainly in Spanish, with some English support. The experienced instructors, particularly Michael and Ami, are praised for their attentiveness and ability to create a calming atmosphere. Participants appreciate the affordability of classes, the availability of rental mats, and the studio's tranquil setting on the upper floors of a building. However, access can be challenging due to the absence of an elevator. Some reviews indicate mixed experiences with staff interactions, emphasizing the importance of friendly service alongside the yoga practice. Overall, it’s a place highly recommended for those looking to engage with yoga and join a supportive community.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and inclusive community atmosphere.
- Experienced and attentive instructors.
- Affordable donation-based classes.
- Calming and tranquil environment.
- Availability of rental mats.

## Areas for Improvement

- Access can be difficult due to the lack of an elevator.
- Mixed experiences with staff interactions.
- Negative feedback about handling of payment issues.
