# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a tranquil and inviting atmosphere, surrounded by nature, making it a perfect retreat in the heart of Ubud. With a diverse range of classes including Vinyasa, Ashtanga, and Kundalini, there is something for every practitioner, from beginners to seasoned yogis. The instructors are highly regarded for their expertise, compassion, and ability to create a welcoming environment. Many reviews highlight intimate class sizes and personalized guidance, although some feedback suggests a desire for more instructor engagement in certain classes. The studio is also appreciated for its beautiful space, natural light, and stunning views. Additionally, the on-site café serves nutritious food, enhancing the overall experience.

## Why You'll Love It

- Tranquil atmosphere surrounded by nature
- Diverse range of yoga classes
- Highly skilled and compassionate instructors
- Intimate class sizes allowing for personalized guidance
- Nutritious food available at the on-site café

## Areas for Improvement

- Some classes perceived as not true to their advertised style
- Lack of instructor engagement in certain classes
- Reception staff sometimes described as curt
