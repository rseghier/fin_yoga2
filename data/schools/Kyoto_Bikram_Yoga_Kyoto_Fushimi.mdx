# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and welcoming atmosphere, perfect for practitioners of all levels. It is especially noted for its cleanliness and well-maintained facilities, providing a beautiful environment to practice Bikram yoga. The staff is friendly and accommodating, showing kindness to visitors regardless of their language skills. Despite the classes being conducted in Japanese, many reviewers appreciated this as it enhanced their experience while visiting Kyoto. Overall, the studio has garnered high praise for its aesthetic appeal, supportive instructors, and quality of yoga practice.



## Why You'll Love It

- Beautifully maintained and clean facilities
- Friendly and accommodating staff
- Supportive atmosphere for non-Japanese speakers
- High-quality yoga experience
- Great shower facilities
