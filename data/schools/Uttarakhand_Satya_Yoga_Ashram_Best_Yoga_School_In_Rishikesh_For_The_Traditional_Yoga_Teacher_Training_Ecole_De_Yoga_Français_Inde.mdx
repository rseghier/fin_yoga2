# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comprehensive and transformative teacher training program focused on authentic Hatha yoga, rooted in traditional practices. Participants praise the highly skilled instructors, particularly Satya, for their compassionate teaching approach and depth of knowledge. Classes include not only physical practice but also philosophy, anatomy, pranayama, and meditation, providing a holistic understanding of yoga. The environment fosters personal growth and community, characterized by supportive interactions among students and faculty. Additionally, many reviews highlight the studio's advantage of offering courses in French, making it accessible to a wider audience. Overall, the studio is described as a nurturing space for yoga practice, learning, and self-discovery in the spiritually rich setting of Rishikesh, India.

With excellent facilities and nourishing meals, it promises a well-rounded and enriching yoga experience for all levels of practitioners.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly skilled and compassionate instructors, especially Satya.
- Comprehensive curriculum covering asanas, philosophy, anatomy, and meditation.
- Supportive and nurturing community atmosphere.
- Classes available in French, enhancing accessibility for French speakers.
- Delicious and nourishing meals that align with the yogic lifestyle.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some participants reported issues with accommodation quality.
- Concerns over class organization and pacing with some instructors.
- A few reviews mentioned overly strict rules during the training schedule.
