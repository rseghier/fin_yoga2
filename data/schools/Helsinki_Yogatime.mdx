# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, making it a popular choice for relaxation and rejuvenation. The small and beautiful yoga space allows for personalized attention during classes, which primarily focus on Vinyasa flow and Yin with sound therapy. Despite the majority of classes being in Russian, there are efforts to introduce more English sessions. The instructors, especially Karina, are praised for their professionalism and warmth, contributing to a positive and motivating environment for practitioners. The unique sound bowl experience adds an extra layer of relaxation, helping attendees unwind from their daily stress. Overall, the studio is recommended for those seeking a soothing yoga experience in a cozy setting.

The studio promotes a community feel and a commitment to offering varied styles, bridging cultural divides and enhancing well-being.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and motivating atmosphere
- Professional and skilled instructors
- Positive community feel
- Unique sound bowl experience for relaxation

## Areas for Improvement

- Majority of classes are in Russian, limited English options
- Limited information about outdoor classes
- Studio hours only available on-site
