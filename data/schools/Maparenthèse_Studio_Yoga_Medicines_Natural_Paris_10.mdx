# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is located in Paris and offers a variety of classes, including yoga and Pilates. However, it has faced significant criticism from clients regarding last-minute cancellations and poor customer service. Many reviewers have expressed frustration over the difficulty in booking sessions and redeeming gift vouchers, with some calling the experience disrespectful and disorganized. When clients attempted to address these issues, they often received no follow-up, leaving them dissatisfied. On a positive note, one review highlighted the quality of the yoga classes, describing them as inspiring and enriching. Additionally, the café associated with the studio has been praised for its friendly service and delicious food. 

Overall, while the studio has potential with great classes, it appears to struggle with consistency and management.

## Why You'll Love It

- Inspiring yoga classes that provide a unique experience.
- Friendly atmosphere in the associated café with quality food and drinks.

## Areas for Improvement

- Frequent last-minute class cancellations.
- Poor communication and customer service regarding bookings.
- Difficulty in redeeming gift vouchers with no follow-up action taken.
