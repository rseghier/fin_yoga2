# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers courses in Yogaterapia and Eutonía but is criticized for its poor organization.
Many students have reported issues with communication, including unanswered messages and delays in receiving feedback.
Additionally, problems have been highlighted regarding the issuance of certificates upon course completion, leading to frustration among participants.
Overall, the perception of the studio is marred by these administrative shortcomings.
