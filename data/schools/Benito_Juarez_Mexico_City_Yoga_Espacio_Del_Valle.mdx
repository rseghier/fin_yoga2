# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a spacious, well-lit, and clean environment ideal for practice. The professional instructors contribute to a pleasant atmosphere, making it a visually appealing space. However, concerns were raised about the demeanor of the person in charge, who was described as inflexible and rude. Issues regarding overcharging were also mentioned, which detracts from the overall positive experience. Despite its beautiful setting, the management experience can negatively impact potential renters.

## Why You'll Love It

- Spacious and well-lit environment
- Clean facilities
- Professional instructors
- Visually appealing space

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor management attitude
- Inflexibility in handling issues
- Incidents of overcharging
- Rude behavior from the person in charge
