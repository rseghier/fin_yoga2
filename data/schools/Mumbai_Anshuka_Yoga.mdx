# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Mumbai is known for its celebrity trainer who is gentle, down-to-earth, and attentive to her students. Many reviews highlight the effective and enjoyable Flyfit yoga classes, along with special retreats and healthy brunches. Despite her celebrity status, the trainer is praised for her personalized approach, making students feel valued. However, the studio's accessibility is a concern, with reports of poor communication and unresponsiveness from the staff, which detracts from the overall experience. The high cost of classes also raised some issues among reviewers. In summary, while the studio offers excellent training and a welcoming atmosphere, the administrative challenges may hinder potential clients.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and gentle celebrity yoga trainer
- Personalized attention to students
- Popular yoga style with effective classes
- Well-organized retreats and healthy brunches
- Positive reputation among participants

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor communication and unresponsive staff
- Accessibility issues with scheduling and inquiries
- High cost of classes
- Perception of exclusivity for celebrities
- Rude receptionists reported
