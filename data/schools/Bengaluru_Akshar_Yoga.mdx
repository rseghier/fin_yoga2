# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a tranquil environment for yoga practice with a strong focus on personal growth and wellness. Many reviewers appreciate the positive vibe of the space and the power of the teachings. However, there are significant concerns about the structure and quality of the teacher training course, which seem to lack depth and proper instruction from experienced teachers. The sessions are often heavily focused on note dictation rather than practical application, leading to frustration among students. Issues such as delayed certification and unfulfilled promises regarding class extensions have also been highlighted. While it may serve well for daily yoga practices, potential students should proceed with caution, especially regarding the teacher training programs.

Overall, the studio is recognized for its peaceful atmosphere but has received criticism for its administrative inconsistencies and lack of practical instruction.

## Why You'll Love It

- Positive and energetic environment
- Great place for daily yoga practice
- Quality instructions from Himalayan Siddha Akshar ji

## Areas for Improvement

- Unstructured teacher training course with little depth
- Sessions often focused on note dictation rather than practical yoga
- Considerable delays in receiving certification
- Miscommunication about course structure and promises
- Non-refundable payment terms for certain classes
