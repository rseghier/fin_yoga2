# Studio Presentation

This yoga and pilates studio in Osaka provides a welcoming and intimate environment, catering especially to travelers. The instructors are known for their friendliness and professional approach, offering classes in both English and Japanese, ensuring inclusivity for all participants. The studio's atmosphere is cozy and calming, often described as an oasis with a warm ambiance that fosters community. A unique travelers pass allows visitors to join unlimited classes for a week, making it ideal for those on short stays. The dedicated owners and instructors pay attention to students' needs, creating a supportive space for both beginners and seasoned yogis. Overall, it’s a beautiful space with excellent instruction that provides a memorable experience for anyone visiting Osaka.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and professional instructors who teach in both English and Japanese.
- Cozy, welcoming atmosphere described as an oasis.
- Special travelers pass for unlimited classes during a week.
- Great attention to students' needs and a supportive community.
- Excellent instruction and a variety of yoga styles available.

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited space, making it hard to accommodate larger classes.
- Some classes are taught primarily in Japanese, which may be challenging for non-speakers.
- The studio can become crowded during peak times.
