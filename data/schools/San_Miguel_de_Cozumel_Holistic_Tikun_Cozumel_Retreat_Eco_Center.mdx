# Studio Presentation

This retreat offers a holistic and immersive experience in a tranquil, natural setting, making it an ideal sanctuary for relaxation and healing. Guests consistently praise the varied and knowledgeable yoga instructors, the delicious plant-based meals, and the warm hospitality of the staff. The property features lush gardens, cozy accommodations, and additional activities like snorkeling and temazcal ceremonies. While the retreat fosters a strong sense of community and support, some guests have experienced issues with cleanliness and accommodation expectations. ## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and welcoming staff who create a family-like atmosphere
- Diverse, knowledgeable yoga instructors provide enriching classes
- Delicious and healthy plant-based meals
- Tranquil and beautiful natural surroundings with a lush garden
- Wide range of wellness activities including massages and snorkeling

## Areas for Improvement

- Issues with cleanliness and maintenance in some accommodations
- Miscommunication regarding room amenities and descriptions
- Some guests reported dissatisfaction with food quality
- Accommodation may be too basic for some guests' expectations
- Volunteer roles may lack clarity, leading to frustration
