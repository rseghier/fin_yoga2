# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio provides a well-rounded education in yoga and holistic practices including meditation, flower remedies, and acupuncture. The instructor, Vidhya, is praised for her deep knowledge and ability to relate lessons to real-life experiences. Students benefit from comprehensive notes and a supportive WhatsApp group that enhances their learning. However, there have been concerns about the irregularity of classes, which has led to dissatisfaction regarding long-term engagement. Overall, it is considered a great place for personal growth and learning, with a positive atmosphere that fosters happiness and change in students' lives.



## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable instructor with real-life examples in teaching.
- Supportive follow-up and resources provided through WhatsApp.
- Comprehensive approach including yoga, meditation, and acupuncture.
- Positive atmosphere that encourages personal growth.

## Areas for Improvement

- Irregular class scheduling and lack of reliability.
- Classes may be withdrawn suddenly without notice.
- Limited long-term commitments reported by students.
