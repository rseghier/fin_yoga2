# Studio Presentation

Located in a convenient area, this yoga studio has a reputation for being clean and well-maintained, although some concerns about hygiene were expressed. Many clients appreciate the friendly and helpful staff, particularly instructors who take the time to correct and assist students during classes. The studio atmosphere is welcoming, making it an appealing option for those looking to practice yoga over a long period. Overall, the commitment to customer service and supportive teaching is a highlight, even with minor issues regarding floor cleanliness. 

It serves as a good community space for yoga enthusiasts, balancing accessibility with a positive teaching environment.

## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and helpful instructors who provide personal attention.
- Convenient location and clean atmosphere.
- Supportive community for long-term yoga practice.

## Areas for Improvement

- Hygiene concerns with equipment like mats and blocks.
- Occasional lapses in staff remembering new clients.
