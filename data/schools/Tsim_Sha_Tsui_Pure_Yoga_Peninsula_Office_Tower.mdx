# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is praised for its friendly and professional staff, including exceptional yoga instructors like Mia, who is highlighted for her dedication and teaching quality. The location offers beautiful views of the harbour, adding to the overall ambiance of the studio. However, there are concerns regarding the maintenance of facilities, particularly the aging changing rooms and shower fixtures. The food service associated with the studio has faced criticism for its inattentive staff and issues with food availability. Overall, the studio provides a positive atmosphere for yoga practice but needs improvements in certain operational aspects.



## Why You'll Love It

- Friendly and professional staff
- Exceptional yoga instructors, especially Mia
- Beautiful views of the harbour
- Great facilities and yoga rooms

## Areas for Improvement

- Aging and poorly maintained changing rooms
- Inattentive food service staff
- Food availability issues
- Poor management of food operations
