# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Ashbourne offers a warm and inviting space for all levels, emphasizing a welcoming atmosphere and attention to detail in decor and lighting. With classes available twice daily, students can expect a variety of offerings, including yoga nidra and meditation. The instructors, particularly Nichola and Deirdre, receive high praise for their interactive and supportive teaching styles, which cater to both adults and children. The studio is equipped with amenities such as available parking and a cosy ambiance that promotes relaxation and community spirit. This studio presents itself as a tranquil oasis ideal for yoga enthusiasts and those seeking mental and physical wellness.
