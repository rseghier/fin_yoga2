# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a vibrant environment for practice, highlighted by professional and friendly instructors who create a welcoming atmosphere. However, customer service appears to be a significant concern, with reports of unresponsive staff and difficulties with class management, including overcrowding. The unique design of some practice spaces, notably the absence of mirrors, encourages participants to focus on their experience rather than their appearance, which can enhance the practice. Despite the positive aspects of the instructors and facilities, the studio's operational issues detract from the overall experience.



## Why You'll Love It

- Professional and friendly instructors
- Welcoming atmosphere
- Unique practice spaces without mirrors

## Areas for Improvement

- Poor customer service
- Overcrowded classes
- Unresponsive staff
