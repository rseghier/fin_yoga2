# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming and supportive environment that fosters personal growth and community. Its spacious and well-maintained facilities provide all necessary equipment free of charge. The atmosphere is described as familial, with instructors who are both friendly and highly qualified. Specializing in Ashtanga Yoga, the studio offers a comprehensive schedule of classes, including beginner courses, Mysore style, and specialized workshops. Students appreciate the individual attention they receive, making it suitable for all levels, including those with injuries. Participants praise their ability to learn and practice yoga effectively, even outside of class, thanks to the clear guidance from the teachers. The beautiful internal garden enhances the serene experience, making each visit feel like a retreat. Overall, students report a strong sense of belonging and satisfaction with their practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and supportive atmosphere
- Highly qualified and friendly instructors
- Comprehensive class schedule including workshops
- Space and tools provided free of charge
- Individual attention for personal practice needs

## Areas for Improvement

- Desire for more variety in evening courses
- Some reviews mention dissatisfaction with clarity in communication
- Concerns raised by a few participants about the studio dynamics
