# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio has received significant negative feedback regarding its customer service and staff professionalism. Many reviews mention a lack of courteous behavior from the staff, particularly highlighting an experience where a sales team was unhelpful and rude. There are also mentions of a stressful atmosphere rather than a peaceful one, with the staff being perceived as impolite and mocking to customers. However, some clients also reflect on the positive aspects of attending classes regularly, suggesting that the studio offers good value for those willing to engage in a consistent practice. The overall sentiment leans towards dissatisfaction with the service experience, overshadowing the potential benefits of the studio's offerings.

These concerns about customer support and staff interaction are crucial for potential clients to consider before joining.

## Why You'll Love It

- Offers a good value for regular classes
- Provides opportunities for daily practice

## Areas for Improvement

- Unprofessional and rude staff
- Mocking attitude from sales team
- Stressful rather than peaceful atmosphere
- Negative client service experiences
