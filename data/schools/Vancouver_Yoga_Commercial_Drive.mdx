# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a spacious and authentic environment for practicing Bikram hot yoga, focused on the original 26 postures and a 90-minute class format. 
The instructors are highly experienced and inspiring, creating a supportive community where practitioners feel rejuvenated after every session.
