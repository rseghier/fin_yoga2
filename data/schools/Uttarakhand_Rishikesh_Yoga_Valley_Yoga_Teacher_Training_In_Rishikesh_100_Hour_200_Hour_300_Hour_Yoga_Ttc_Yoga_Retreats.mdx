# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a comprehensive range of teacher training programs, including 100, 200, and 300-hour courses, set in a serene natural environment in Rishikesh. The key instructors, Kamal and Harshita, are respected for their deep knowledge of yoga and Ayurveda, providing a nurturing and supportive atmosphere for students. Reviews highlight the personalized attention from teachers in small class sizes and emphasize the focus on proper alignment and safety in yoga practice. Fresh, healthy vegetarian food is served, enhancing the overall wellness experience. The studio is not marketed as an ashram, allowing for a more relaxed setting while still maintaining a high standard of yoga education. Engagement in community activities and excursions adds a unique dimension to the training experience, making it not just educational but transformative.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and supportive teachers, particularly Kamal and Harshita.
- Healthy, fresh, vegetarian meals that enhance the experience.
- Personalized attention due to small class sizes.
- A tranquil environment ideal for yoga practice and personal growth.
- Well-structured courses covering various styles and philosophies.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some negative experiences regarding miscommunication and professionalism.
- Misleading marketing regarding accommodations as an ashram.
- Occasional conflicts between expectations and actual experiences.
