# Studio Presentation

Located in Montreal, this yoga studio provides a unique blend of yoga and fitness, attracting clients with its warm and welcoming atmosphere. The studio is well-regarded for its cleanliness, beautiful design, and modern amenities, including FAR infrared heat therapy that enhances the yoga experience. Instructors like Annie have been highlighted for their exceptional teaching skills, making classes accessible and enjoyable for all levels. Clients appreciate the variety of classes offered, from hot yoga and yin-yang sessions to pilates and specialized fitness classes. This studio strives to create an inclusive setting where participants can connect with their practice and unwind effectively.

Overall, it’s a favorite among visitors for its friendly staff, effective workouts, and convenient location in Old Port.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and welcoming atmosphere with friendly staff
- Clean and well-designed space
- Variety of classes including yoga, pilates, and fitness
- Effective use of FAR infrared technology for hot yoga
- Exceptional instructors, especially Annie

## Areas for Improvement

- Strict late entry policy for heated sessions
- Limited information available on website about classes
- Only available experience was with the front desk staff
