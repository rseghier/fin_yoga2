# Studio Presentation

Located in Chennai, this yoga studio offers a diverse range of classes suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. The trainers are highly skilled, particularly Bipin, who is praised for his personalized attention and ability to motivate students. The atmosphere is welcoming, fostering a sense of community among participants. While the quality of instruction remains high, there have been concerns regarding the maintenance and quality of equipment, particularly in aerial yoga classes. This studio emphasizes mindfulness and physical challenge, creating a rejuvenating experience for attendees. It's a great place for those looking to improve their strength and flexibility in a supportive environment.



## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and knowledgeable instructors, especially Bipin.
- Welcoming and inclusive atmosphere fostering community.
- Variety of yoga styles offered catering to all levels.
- Focus on mindfulness and individual progression.
- Systematic and scientific approach to yoga training.

## Areas for Improvement

- Degradation in the quality of some yoga equipment.
- Lack of special advanced workshops for long-term members.
- Reduction in hygiene practices, such as the discontinuation of antibacterial spray.
- Limited availability of certain yoga styles like rope yoga.
