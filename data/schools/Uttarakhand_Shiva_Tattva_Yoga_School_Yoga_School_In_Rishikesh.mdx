# Studio Presentation

Located in the serene backdrop of the mountains, this yoga studio offers a unique blend of personal growth and professional training. With a focus on wellness, the studio provides extensive yoga teacher training programs, including 200-hour courses, that emphasize both asana practice and the philosophy of yoga. Students appreciate the supportive and friendly staff who foster a family-like atmosphere. Many reviews highlighted the delicious, healthy food and comfortable accommodations that enhance the overall experience. However, students noted areas for improvement, particularly in communication and the need for more hands-on adjustments during classes. Despite some challenges, attendees felt their training was enriching and transformative.

The studio is also praised for its beautiful practice space and proximity to nature, creating an uplifting environment conducive to learning and personal development.

## Why You'll Love It

- Supportive and friendly staff creating a family-like atmosphere
- Delicious and healthy food provided on-site
- Comfortable accommodations in a tranquil setting
- Highly qualified yoga instructors with expertise in various styles
- Focus on both yoga practice and philosophy education

## Areas for Improvement

- Communication could be improved, especially regarding teacher absences
- Some teachers showed a lack of energy and motivation
- Need for more hands-on adjustments during classes
- Occasional disparity in opinions among staff affecting the environment
- Limited modifications offered during sessions
