# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a variety of classes including Hatha Vinyasa and Aerial Yoga, providing a balanced mix of strength and relaxation. The instructors, Komang and Putra, are known for their clear and attentive guidance, ensuring that each student receives personalized feedback. Set in a cozy rooftop environment, it creates a friendly and welcoming atmosphere for practitioners of all skill levels, including beginners. They focus on individual adjustments, making classes safe and supportive. Facilities include water, mats, and towels, contributing to a comfortable experience. Many reviews highlight the beauty of the space and the authentic Balinese atmosphere, making it a perfect spot to escape into yoga practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Experienced and attentive instructors who provide personalized feedback.
- Cozy and beautiful rooftop environment enhancing the yoga experience.
- Classes suitable for all levels, with a focus on individual needs.
- Clear instructions and adjustments from teachers during sessions.
- Affordable pricing for quality yoga classes.

## Areas for Improvement

- Some reviews indicated an inconsistent quality of instruction for beginners.
- A few visitors faced issues with booking and class scheduling.
- Class sizes can be limited, making advance booking necessary.
