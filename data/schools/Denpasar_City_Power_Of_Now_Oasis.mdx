# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a beautiful escape set in a serene environment right by the beach in Denpasar City, Bali. With two studios housed in a bamboo structure surrounded by nature, it emphasizes a tranquil and peaceful atmosphere. The studio hosts classes every morning and evening without requiring reservations, encouraging a flexible drop-in approach. Friendly and knowledgeable instructors provide a variety of yoga styles, catering to different skill levels. Students can enjoy the sounds of nature during their practice, including waves and birds. The space is well-maintained, and props are provided. However, some visitors have noted issues with specific classes and the need for better signage regarding safety around the resident cow. Overall, it's praised for its relaxing ambiance, skilled teachers, and convenient location, making it a popular choice for both locals and tourists.

## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful, serene location by the beach
- Knowledgeable and friendly instructors
- Variety of classes catering to all skill levels
- Clean and well-maintained facilities
- Flexible drop-in classes without reservations

## Areas for Improvement

- Some classes can be crowded, especially on weekends
- Misleading titles for specific classes
- Concerns about safety around the resident cow
- Inconsistent quality of certain classes
- Limited guidance for beginners during classes
