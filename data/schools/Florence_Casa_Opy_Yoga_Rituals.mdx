# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and welcoming environment amidst the bustling city. It boasts a variety of classes, including vinyasa and unique combinations of myofascial techniques and yin, led by knowledgeable and caring instructors. The owner, Livia, brings a rich cultural background, blending Indian and Brazilian influences into her teaching. Students appreciate the inclusive atmosphere, making it accessible for all levels. Beyond regular yoga classes, workshops and rituals, including cacao ceremonies and sound baths, provide enriching experiences. The studio is celebrated for its beautiful energy and community-driven approach, fostering a deep connection to both body and spirit.
