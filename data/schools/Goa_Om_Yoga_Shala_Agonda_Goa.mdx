# Studio Presentation

Nestled in the serene jungle of south Goa, this yoga studio offers a tranquil retreat with a deep commitment to yoga philosophy and practice. Guests appreciate the knowledgeable teachers, Sanjeev and Shivanni, who create a welcoming environment, catering to all skill levels and offering personalized instruction. The studio features immersive classes, including Ashtanga and theory sessions, designed to enrich both physical practice and understanding of yoga principles. While the venue is praised for its natural beauty and friendly atmosphere, some guests have expressed concerns about cleanliness and the quality of meals. Nevertheless, the community feeling, surrounded by nature and like-minded individuals, makes for a memorable experience. Participants have noted their personal growth and the supportive environment fostered by both staff and fellow attendees, making this studio a transformative space for many.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and supportive teachers with deep understanding of yoga philosophy.
- Inviting and friendly atmosphere that fosters a sense of community.
- Beautiful natural setting in the jungle close to the beach.
- Variety of yoga classes catering to different skill levels.
- Delicious and predominantly vegan meals provided by an accommodating chef.

## Areas for Improvement

- Cleanliness issues reported regarding accommodations.
- Inconsistent quality of food especially after kitchen staff changes.
- Some guests found the location too far from the beach.
- Reports of a noisy environment due to nearby roads and wildlife.
- Confusion regarding itinerary and structure for newcomers.
