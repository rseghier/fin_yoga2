# Studio Presentation

This serene homestay offers a tranquil getaway nestled in the forest, close to Patnam Beach. Guests rave about the excellent hospitality from the host, Pooja, who provides a welcoming and nurturing environment. With spacious and clean accommodations, including access to cooking facilities and yoga sessions, it is an ideal retreat for nature lovers and those looking for relaxation and wellness. The property features a yoga room with beautiful surroundings that enhances the overall calming atmosphere. Guests appreciate the home-cooked meals and the opportunity to practice yoga and meditation daily. The proximity to local attractions while being secluded in nature makes it a perfect escape for a peaceful vacation.

## Why You'll Love It

- Welcoming and attentive host who provides excellent hospitality.
- Spacious, clean rooms with kitchen facilities.
- Serene location surrounded by nature, ideal for relaxation and yoga.
- Home-cooked meals praised by guests.
- Daily yoga and meditation classes offered.

## Areas for Improvement

- Location can be hard to reach via dirt roads.
- Some amenities like water flow in the bathroom need attention.
- Limited accessibility for those without personal transport.
- Occasional absence of yoga instructors.
- Walking to nearby shops/restaurants may be inconvenient for some.
