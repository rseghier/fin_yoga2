# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio in Pyrmont offers a unique Mysore Ashtanga experience that differs from typical yoga classes. The environment promotes dedication and personal growth, emphasizing a customized practice tailored to each individual's journey. The holistic approach to yoga covers not just physical postures but integrates mind, body, and spirit. Reviewers appreciate the supportive and non-competitive atmosphere, making it suitable for practitioners of all levels, including those with chronic injuries. The main teacher, Lainie, is highly praised for her warmth and commitment, fostering a strong sense of community among students. The studio strives for a balance between challenge and self-compassion, encouraging a lifelong learning experience in yoga.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly personalized practice tailored to individual needs
- Supportive and non-competitive atmosphere
- Holistic approach integrating mind, body, and spirit
- Exceptional teacher fostering community and encouragement
- Accommodates students of all ages and skill levels

## Areas for Improvement

- Access involves multiple sets of staircases
- Some reviews indicate difficulty attending regularly due to health issues
