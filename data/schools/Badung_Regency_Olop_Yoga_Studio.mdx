# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a tranquil and welcoming atmosphere ideal for both beginners and more experienced practitioners. The focus is on Iyengar Yoga, which emphasizes alignment and the use of props to enhance postural practice. Classes are led by a knowledgeable teacher who provides clear guidance and supportive adjustments. The studio fosters a sense of community, where participants can engage with each other over tea post-class, enhancing the social experience. Despite a rich learning environment, some reviews indicate that beginners might find the class levels challenging, and there may be a sense of cliquishness among long-term attendees. Overall, the studio is praised for its clean facilities and dedicated instructors, making it a good choice for committed yoga practitioners.

## Why You'll Love It

- Tranquil atmosphere that promotes peace and focus.
- Knowledgeable instructor who provides helpful adjustments.
- Strong sense of community with post-class socializing.
- Clean facilities and good quality equipment.
- Responsive communication via WhatsApp.

## Areas for Improvement

- Classes can be challenging for beginners.
- Some long-term members create a sense of exclusivity.
- Not welcoming to local tourists in some instances.
- Mosquito issues noted during sessions.
- High prices compared to the service received.
