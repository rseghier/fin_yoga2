# Studio Presentation

This yoga school offers an immersive teacher training experience focused on the essence of yoga. Students can expect a highly professional and supportive environment where individualized guidance is a priority, accommodating both beginners and experienced practitioners. The courses are complemented by a strong emphasis on proper alignment, yoga philosophy, and anatomy, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of the practice. Highly positive interactions with teachers, who become friends along the journey, enhance the learning experience. The nurturing atmosphere facilitates personal growth and a sense of community among participants. Delicious and nutritious meals add to the overall well-being during training, making it a memorable experience.

The program combines various styles including Ashtanga and includes unique activities like sound healing and dance improvisation, creating a rich tapestry of learning opportunities.
