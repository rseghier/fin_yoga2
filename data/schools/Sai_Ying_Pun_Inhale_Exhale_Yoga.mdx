Inhale Exhale Yoga is a boutique yoga studio located in Sai Ying Pun, Hong Kong.

**Address:** Room J, 1/F, Fook Moon Building, No. 56-72 Third St, Sai Ying Pun, Hong Kong

**Category:** Yoga studio

# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a clean and spacious environment, ideal for both relaxation and physical activity. With professional instructors, particularly praised is Doris, the studio can cater to both beginners and experienced practitioners. The blend of indoor and outdoor spaces creates a unique atmosphere for mindfulness and engagement. Students enjoy the convenience of the location and the reasonable class packages available. The small size of the studio provides a boutique experience that many find lacking in larger chains, contributing to a tight-knit community feel. Positive transformations in health and well-being are frequently reported among attendees, highlighting the effectiveness of the instructors and programs offered.



## Why You'll Love It

- Clean and spacious environment for yoga and Pilates.
- Professional and friendly instructors, especially praised is Doris.
- Convenient location with a unique indoor/outdoor setup.
- Affordable class packages and free spring water for students.
- Positive impact on health and well-being, with noticeable improvements reported by attendees.

## Areas for Improvement

- The studio is petite and lacks amenities found in larger studios.
- Instances of disorganization and rude behavior reported by some.
