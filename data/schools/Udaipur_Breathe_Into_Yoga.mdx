# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio is recognized for having highly skilled instructors, particularly noted for their teaching. However, client experiences have raised concerns regarding professionalism and communication. A number of reviews highlight issues with fee management and slot allocation, where clients found themselves without proper notification or support after having prepaid for classes. This lack of transparency and care in administration has left some clients feeling disillusioned, emphasizing the importance of clear policies and ethical practices in managing student agreements.

Overall, while the quality of instruction might be commendable, serious attention needs to be given to operational ethics to enhance client trust and satisfaction.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly skilled yoga instructors
- Notable quality of teaching

## Areas for Improvement

- Unethical management practices
- Poor communication regarding class slots
- Lack of transparency about fee policies
- Non-refundable fees without prior knowledge
- Inadequate client support after payment
