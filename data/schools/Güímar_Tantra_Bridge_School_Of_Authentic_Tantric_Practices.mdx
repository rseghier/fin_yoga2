Tantra Bridge is a retreat center located in Güímar, Santa Cruz de Tenerife, specializing in Tantric practices.

**Address:** Pje. las Rosas, 38500 Güímar, Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Spain

**Category:** Yoga retreat center

# Studio Presentation

This school offers a unique and transformative experience centered around Tantric practices. The welcoming hosts, Stella and Ivan, create a warm atmosphere where guests feel at home. The retreats focus on a combination of yoga, meditation, and Tantric teachings, often set against breathtaking natural backdrops, including views of mountains and the sea. Attendees participate in hands-on learning, including massage techniques and exploring personal growth. Delicious vegetarian meals sourced from local gardens add to the enriching experience. Guests often highlight the supportive environment and the opportunity for personal insights, making each retreat a meaningful journey into Tantra and inner peace.

From engaging activities like outdoor yoga and hikes to relaxing sauna sessions, the retreats cater to a holistic experience that fosters community and connection.

## Why You'll Love It

- Warm and welcoming hosts, creating a home-like atmosphere.
- Well-structured retreats focusing on practical learning and personal growth.
- Beautiful natural surroundings enhancing the overall experience.
- Delicious vegetarian meals prepared with love, often from local sources.
- Diverse activities including yoga, meditation, and outdoor excursions.

## Areas for Improvement

- Basic accommodation may not meet all luxury expectations.
- Cooler temperatures at night could require additional clothing.
- Limited capacity per retreat may restrict availability.
