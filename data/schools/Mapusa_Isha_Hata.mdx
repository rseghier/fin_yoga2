# Studio Presentation

The studio offers a unique and uplifting atmosphere for Hatha Yoga practice. Led by the highly praised instructor Shrinivas Anna, students feel a profound sense of connection and lightness during their sessions. The space is consecrated and embodies a spiritual ambiance that enhances the yoga experience. Practitioners have the opportunity to engage in Surya Kriya and Bhutashuddhi practices, which add depth to their journey. The supportive and cooperative nature of the teaching staff further enriches the practice environment, making it an exceptional place for both newcomers and seasoned yogis. Overall, the studio stands out for its dedication to traditional practices and the spiritual guidance provided.
