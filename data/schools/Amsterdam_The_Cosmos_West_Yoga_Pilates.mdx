# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and beautiful space in the heart of Amsterdam, designed to provide tranquility amidst the urban buzz. The classes range from Strong Vinyasa Flow to Pilates, featuring skilled and attentive instructors like Alicia and Louise, who deliver clear and inspiring guidance. The dim lighting creates an introspective atmosphere perfect for deep practice. The studio also prides itself on cleanliness with convenient mat cleaning setups and high-quality equipment. Community feedback highlights the friendly and caring nature of the staff, making every visit a rejuvenating experience. Students appreciate the variety of classes and the inviting, cozy environment that encourages both physical challenge and relaxation.



## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful and serene atmosphere for practice
- Skilled and supportive instructors
- Variety of classes including Strong Vinyasa Flow and Pilates
- High-quality equipment and cleanliness
- Community-oriented with caring staff

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited space with mats placed close together
- Single room for practice may feel crowded
- No significant weaknesses mentioned
