# Studio Presentation

Nestled in a quiet corner of Canggu, this yoga studio offers a serene atmosphere perfect for both beginners and experienced practitioners. Known for its dedicated and knowledgeable instructors, the studio provides a variety of classes, including Vinyasa, Hatha, and teacher training programs that immerse students in yoga philosophy and practice. Reviewers commend the community vibe created by both staff and students, making it feel like a supportive family. The facilities include a refreshing sauna and ice bath, enhancing the overall wellness experience. Every aspect, from the stunning views to the nourishing meals offered during training, contributes to a transformative and enriching journey for all who attend.

## Why You'll Love It

- Knowledgeable and supportive instructors who create a welcoming environment.
- Diverse range of yoga styles and training programs available.
- Community-focused atmosphere that fosters connection among participants.
- Clean facilities with additional wellness amenities like sauna and ice bath.
- Beautiful, calming location with scenic views.

## Areas for Improvement

- Occasional issues with class availability or studio access.
- Some students felt misled about accommodation provided during training.
- Language barriers can create challenges for non-English speakers.
