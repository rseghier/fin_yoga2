# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a unique and immersive experience surrounded by a lush forest and garden, creating a serene atmosphere for practice. Featuring a variety of classes including barre strong, students appreciate the thoughtful and attentive instructors who cater to diverse skill levels. The studio provides a welcoming environment, emphasizing safety and inclusivity. With a monthly tourist pass, visitors can enjoy unlimited classes, allowing them to deepen their practice over time. Special workshops, such as those organized for International Yoga Day, add to the studio's vibrant community spirit. Additionally, the studio is dog-friendly and equipped with excellent props and a convenient app for class bookings.
