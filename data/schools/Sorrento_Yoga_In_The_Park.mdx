**Address:** opposite Hotel Sorrento, 10 Hotham Rd, Sorrento VIC 3943, Australia

**Category:** Yoga studio

**Score:** 5 based on 12 reviews

**Website:** http://www.yogainthepark.net.au/

# Studio Presentation

This studio offers a unique outdoor yoga experience, allowing practitioners to connect with nature in a tranquil park setting.
The classes cater to various skill levels, promoting a welcoming atmosphere for all participants.
Instructors are praised for their supportive and knowledgeable approaches, enhancing the overall practice and ensuring individual attention.
Moreover, the community vibe encourages regular attendance and fosters friendships among members.
However, the location can become crowded at times, impacting the serenity of practice.

## Why You'll Love It

- Unique outdoor yoga experience
- Welcoming atmosphere for all skill levels
- Supportive and knowledgeable instructors
- Strong sense of community
- Encourages regular attendance and friendships

## Areas for Improvement

- Can become crowded at times
- Outdoor setting may be affected by weather conditions
