# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers an immersive and authentic experience deeply rooted in the traditions of yoga. Nestled in a peaceful location just minutes from the Ganga River, it combines personal attention with professional, skilled teaching from a supportive staff. Students feel at home in a warm, family-like atmosphere, making lifelong friendships during their training. The small class sizes allow for detailed guidance in both physical practices and theoretical learning, enhancing the overall educational experience. Unique excursions, including trips to the Himalayas, enrich the curriculum. The studio promotes a serene escape from the hectic city life, fostering a focus on personal growth and yoga training success.

Overall, this studio is well-loved by its students, who highlight the excellent quality of food, nurturing environment, and knowledgeable instructors, making it a standout choice for aspiring yoga teachers.

## Why You'll Love It

- Highly knowledgeable and supportive instructors
- Warm and family-like atmosphere
- Excellent food offerings prepared with care
- Small class sizes allowing for personal attention
- Unique excursions adding to the comprehensive training experience

## Areas for Improvement

- Facility cleanliness issues reported by some students
- Tuition fees perceived as high compared to other schools
