# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a serene and welcoming atmosphere, providing instruction in English. 
It features a variety of classes including hatha, yin, and vinyasa, catering to both energetic and restorative practices. 
The studio is praised for its beautiful aesthetics and calming presence, making it an enjoyable place for yoga enthusiasts. 
Packs of 'happy hour' classes and free park yoga sessions add to its appeal. 
While it provides quality practices for beginners and intermediate practitioners, those seeking advanced growth might find opportunities limited. 
Facilities include a unisex changing room and the ability to borrow yoga mats at no extra charge.

## Why You'll Love It

- Beautiful and harmonious studio environment
- Wide variety of classes including hatha, yin, and vinyasa
- Affordable pricing with 'happy hour' classes and free summer park yoga
- Good quality instruction with a calming presence
- Access to yoga equipment without extra charges

## Areas for Improvement

- Limited facilities with no shower available
- Only one unisex changing room
- More experienced yogis may find development opportunities lacking
