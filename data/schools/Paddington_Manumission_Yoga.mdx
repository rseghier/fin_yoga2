# Studio Presentation

This yoga studio offers a welcoming atmosphere with dedicated and attentive teachers who create an energizing and calming environment for students. With a variety of teaching styles, the studio caters to both beginners and experienced yogis. Many reviews highlight the phenomenal teaching quality, specifically mentioning one teacher's ability to instill peace and energy in the classes. However, there are significant concerns regarding hygiene practices, particularly with shared mats and props, which some students described as unclean and poorly maintained. Despite its beautiful location, the accumulation of dust has caused allergic reactions for some attendees, leading to frustration over the studio's lack of cleanliness protocols after classes. Overall, while the teaching quality receives high praise, its hygiene practices require urgent improvement.

## Why You'll Love It

- Excellent and attentive teaching staff.
- Positive and calming atmosphere.
- Wide variety of styles suitable for all skill levels.
- Strong community vibe with welcoming teachers.
- Efforts to remain true to yogic principles.

## Areas for Improvement

- Serious cleanliness issues regarding shared props and mats.
- Dusty environment causing allergic reactions.
- Unprofessional treatment of students regarding lateness.
- No post-class hygiene protocols in place.
- Negative studio response to constructive criticism.
