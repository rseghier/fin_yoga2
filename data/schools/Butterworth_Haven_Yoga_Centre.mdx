# Studio Presentation

The yoga studio has received negative feedback related to its customer service and inclusivity. A review highlighted a discriminatory experience when a potential customer was questioned about their ethnicity and faced communication barriers due to language. Such incidents can create an unwelcoming atmosphere for clients.

The overall impression from the review suggests that inclusivity and respect for diverse backgrounds may not be strong points for this studio, potentially deterring a more diverse clientele.
